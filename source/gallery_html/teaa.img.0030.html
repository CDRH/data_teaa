Description

Wearing an a-la-mode dress, hat, and parasol, "Miss Ethiopia" speculates about how the United States government will treat African Americans. The caption reads, "I wonder what they are saying about me"—likely a reference to "What Are the Wild Waves Saying?" (1850), a song by Stephen Glover based upon a scene in Charles Dickens's novel Dombey and Son.

– Nadia Nurhussein
