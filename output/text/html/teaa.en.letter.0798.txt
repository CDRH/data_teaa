







Department of State Received Nov 30 1886

No 12.

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
30th September 1886

Sir

I have the honour to 
acknowledge your circular despatch 
of July 26th last, conveying 
the sad intelligence of the death
of Mr Wm Hunter, the Second
Assistant Secretary of State.

I beg respectfully to sympathize
with the Department in the loss
of so old and able a colleague.

Your instructions as to display
of symbols of mourning are obeyed.

I have the honour to be
Sir
Your most obedient servant
Robt S. Newton
Vice Consul

To Honble F F Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington 
D.C.
