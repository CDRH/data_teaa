







No 109.

United States Consulate
Zanzibar, December 27, 1886
To the Hon
Third Assit. Sec'y of State.
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to
receive the Department's despatches
Nos. 47-48-49 inclosing
a letter to the Sultan, which
has been forwarded, and a form
of a Bond which I have fully
executed and inclose herewith

I have the honor to be, Sir,
Your obedient Servant
E. D. Roper Jr
Act'g U.S. Consul.

Enclosure

1/ Bond, executed
