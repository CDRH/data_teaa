







On the twenty fourth day of November 1859
before me the undersigned personally appeared 
Daniel R. B. Upton Esquire
United States Consul at Bathurst and 
declared that the accounts annexed are
a full &amp; perfect transcript of the register
which he is by law required to :
that the same is true and correct and 
contains a full and accurate statement
of all fees received by him as such
Consular officer, or for his use, for his official 
services to the best of his knowledge, during 
the period of time therein mentioned. 

Danl R. B. Upton
United States Consul

Declared to before me, 
Rich Vine
Police Magistrate
