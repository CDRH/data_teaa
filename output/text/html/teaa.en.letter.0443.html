Department of State Received Sep 17 1883 No 49. Consulate of the United States of America at St Paul de Loanda S.W. Coast of Africa. Ls de R. du Verge to State Dept. Washington D.C. Subject Congo River &a &a Encloses Statement of Affairs and abuses arising then. United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda S.W.C. Africa July 7th 1883. To Hon John Davis Asst. Secty. of State Washington D.C. Sir I have the honor respectfully to enclose herewith memorandum of the Affair, Slaves &a &a are conducted bought sold &a on the Congo and at the principal factories of the Left and right land I have the honor to be Sir Your obt servt Ls de R. du Verge US Consul "One Enclosure"
