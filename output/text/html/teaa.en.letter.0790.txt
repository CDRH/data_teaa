







Department of State Received Nov 28 1885

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
10 October 1885

Sir

I have the honor to
acknowledge receipt of Circular
announcing the death of of
Ex president General Ulysses S. Grant
and beg to tender my deep regret
to the Department for the loss of a 
so great and gallant citizen

I have further the honor to
inform the Department that the
usual symbols of mourning were
assumed at this Consulate according
to instructions

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your most obedt Servt
Wm S. Bent
Acting Vice Consul

To the 
Secretary of State
Washington
D.C.
