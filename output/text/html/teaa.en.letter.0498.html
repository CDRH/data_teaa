Department of State Received Jun 13 1888 No. 36. U.S. Consulate, St. Paul de Loanda, March 21, 1888. Mr. Downing To the Department of State Subject: Coin of the Congo Free State Abstract of contents: Advise of sending samples of above named coin to department. No. 36. U.S. Consulate St. Paul de Loanda March 31, 1888 Hon G.L. Rives, Asst Secretary of State, Washington, D.C. Sir: I have the honor to inform the Department that I have forwarded, by mail, this date, samples of the new coinage of the Congo Free State to the value of $5.00/100 I have the honor to be, Sir, Your obedient servant Henry F. Downing U.S. Consul
