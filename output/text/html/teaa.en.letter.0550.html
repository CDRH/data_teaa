No. 28. Department of State Received Jun 22 1891 Consulate of the United States, Loanda, April 29, 1891 Mr. Bannister. To the Department of State. Subject: Reply to India Rubber Circular of September 20, 1890. Abstract of Contents. Transmitting report on the production of Crude India Rubber in the Province of Angola, and vicinity. 1 Inclosure No. 28. Consulate of the United States, Loanda, April 29, 1891. Honorable W. F. Wharton Assistant Secretary of State, Washington, D.C. Sir: In compliance with the instructions conveyed in the Circular dated 20 September 1890, I have the honor to transmit herewith a "Report on the production of Crude India Rubber" in the Province of Angola and its dependencies. The delay in forwarding it is owing to want of quick communication with the sources of information, and certain difficulty in getting at statistics. I have the honor to be Sir, Your obedient servant Ed Bannister Vice Consul.
