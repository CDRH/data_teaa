







Department of State Received Aug 22 1885

Philada Pa. Aug 20th 1885

Hon Thos F. Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Dr Sir

Appointed U.S. Consul
at St Paul de Loando. I left this country for
my post of duty last December, but on reaching
England, continued ill health obliged me 
to return to the United States.

Having regained my health, I am now
prepared to proceed to Loando and assume
charge of the U.S. Consulate if agreeable to
your Department.

I have the honor to be
Your obt. servant
Robt. H. Davis
U.S. Consul
