Recd Oct. 5 Ack Oct 9 No 9 United States Commercial Agency St Paul de Loanda July 28th 1865. Sir, I have the honor to invite the attention of the Department to my request in Despatch No. 2 for an additional supply of Blanks Nos 44, 14, 8, 142, 143, 144, 130, respectively I would also ask the Department to furnish me with two flags as those now in use are considerably worn. I would like to have them of a large size say 15 x 10 feet, as my residence requires one of that size. I have the honor to be Your most Obdt Servt Augustus Archer Silva Commercial Agent Hon. William H. Seward Secretary of State Washington
