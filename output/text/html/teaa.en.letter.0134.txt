No 80

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
30th September 1881

Sir,

I have the honor to 
acknowledge receipt of the 
Revised Consular Regulations
of 1881 and one Consular Flag
forwarded to me by B. F. Stevens
of London. I have also duly
received Circulars dated May
1st and June 20th and Circular
dated July 25th with reference 
to the new Consular Regulations
which has my attention.

I have the honor to be 
Sir
Your most Obedient Servant
Robt S Newton
Vice Consul

To the 
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
