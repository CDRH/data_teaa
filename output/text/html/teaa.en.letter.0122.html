Ackgd June 18 Department of State Received Jun 14 1879 No. 40 United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda Mr Andrew Galbraith Colville To the Department of State Subject. Advising taking over charge of this consulate from Mr. Robert Scott Newton. No. 40 United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda 23rd April 1879 Sir, I have the honor to inform the Department that on account of Mr. Robert Scott Newton's departure for Europe I have this day taken over charge of this consulate together with the archives records & other effects. I have the honor to be Sir Your most obedient servant And. G. Colville Acting Vice Consul To the Assistant Secretary of State Washington D.C.
