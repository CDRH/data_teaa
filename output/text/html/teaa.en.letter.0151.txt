







State Department Received Oct 7 1882

No 30 11

United States Consulate
at St Paul de Loanda

L. de R. du Verge
to Dept. of State

Subject

Consider Regulations, Flags &amp;a.

Encloses

Request and purpose for the same







United States Consulate
St. Paul de Loanda
Augt. 12th 1882

To
Honorable J.C. Bancroft Davis
Asst. Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor most respectfully to 
request that blank forms and stationary 
be forwarded to this consulate as soon as
possible besides Eight U.S. Flags; Two
for this consulate which is in need of the 
same, and six other for the Consular 
Agents on this coast, together with six
Consular Regulations; for which this
consulate will be responsible.

I would also respectfully aske that 
another U.S. Coat of arms be forwarded
to me, as the old one is now unfit for
use

I have the honor to be Sir
Respectfully
Your obedient servant
L de R du Verge
US Consul
