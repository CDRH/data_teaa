Department of State Received Mar 18 1882 United States Consulate at St Paul de Loanda W.C.A. March 10th 1882 L. de R. du Verge to Dept of State Subject. Acceptance of Appointment Encloses Oath of office and official bond. No 2 United States Consulate at St Paul de Loanda W.C.A. March 10th 1882 The Honorable J. C. Bancroft Davis Asst Sect. of State Washington D.C. Sir I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of formal appointment paper Ns 1, 2, & 3, together with forms of oath, and official bonds. In reply I have the honor to hereby accept the office of Consul of the United States at St Paul de Loanda. I have also to state that I was born in the Isle of France, that I am a naturalized citizen of the U.S. Evidence of which I have lodged in the Dept. and that I am appointed to my present office from the state of Maryland and my residence is the city of Baltimore and that I have never resided in Portugal or any of its dependencies. I have the honor to be Sir Your obt svt L. de R. du Verge Enclosures Offical Bond and oath of office.
