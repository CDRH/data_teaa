Department of State Received Jul 1 1887 No 3. United States Consulate. Saint Paul de Loando New York, June 30th 1887. Mr H. F. Downing To the Department of State. Washington, D.C. Subject. Reporting departure from the United States for post. Abstract of contents. Same as above. No 3. United States Consulate. Saint Paul de Loando June 30th 1887. Hon. Jas. D. Porter, Asst. Secretary of State, Washington, D.C. Sir: I have the honor to inform you that I have this day commenced my transit, and will proceed to my post via Liverpool, England. I have the honor to be, Sir, Your obedient servant, Henry F Downing. Consul.
