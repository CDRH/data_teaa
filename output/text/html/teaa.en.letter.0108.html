Ack. Apl. 17. 77 Department of State Received Apr 15 1877 No. 122 United States Commercial Agency at St Paul de Loanda To the Department of State Mr. Robert Scott Newton Subject. Coasting Trade between the Ports of Angola Abstract of Contents Enclosing translation of circular from the Governor General intimating that no interference will take place on the part of the Government with respect to the Coasting Trade, by Foreign vessels. No. 122 United States Commercial Agency St Paul de Loanda 19th February 1877 Sir I have the honor to enclose the Translation of a Circular dated 10th February 1877 received from His Excellency the Governor General of this Province intimating that no interference will take place on the part of the Government in reference to the Coasting Trade between the Ports of the Province of Angola, referred to in my despatch No 116. I have the honor to be Your most obedt Servant Robt S. Newton Vice Commercial Agent To the Assistant Secretary of State Washington, D.C. Illustrious Sir, His Excellency the Governor General, has instructed me to advise you that His Majesty's Government in order to encourage commerce, permits small vessels, with foreign flags, to trade on the Coast of this Province as previous to the publication of the provincial order No. 404 of the 21st September of the past year God guard you. Office of the Secretary General in Loanda 10th February 1877 To the Vice Commercial Agent of the United States (Signed) Custodio Jose Duarte Sec: Genl
