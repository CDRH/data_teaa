Department of State Received Jan 16 1871 United States Consulate Bathurst River Gambia 25th November 1870 Sir I have to inform you that, I have received officially from the Administrator of these Settlements copy of Her Britannic Majesty's order in Council dated, Windsor 17th day of August 1870 bearing reference to the convention between Great Britain and the United States under the "Naturalization act 1870" and shall be glad to receive any instructions you may deem necessary for my guidance I am Sir Your obedient Servant Thomas Brown Consul pro tem To The Honorable Hamilton Fish Secretary of State Washington
