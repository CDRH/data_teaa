







Department of State Received Dec 13 1887

No. 9

United States Consulate,
St. Paul de Loanda
Oct 19th 1887.

Mr Downing
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Transmitting Exchange voucher.

Abstract of Contents:

Same as above.



United States Consulate,
St Paul de Loanda,
Oct 19th 1887.

Hon. James D. Porter,
Assistant Secretary of States
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to enclose
herewith Exchance voucher, (Form 
No. 92.), being cost of local currency 
when bought by money of the
United States.

In connection with the above I 
would state that it is very difficult
to negotiate consular drafts here. No
parties in Loanda doing business
with the United States, and I am
informed that if in the future I 
wish to dispose of my drafts a higher
rate of exchange will be charged.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
Henry F. Downing, Consul
