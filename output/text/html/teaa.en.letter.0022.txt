







Salem Mass July 1, 1861

Sir,

I now have the honor to inform 
you that I have arrived at this Port from St. 
Paul d Loanda.

By refering to my No 14 enclosed, Your 
Honor will perceive I am on Leave of Absence,
&amp; if it should be the desire of the State or Navy Departments that I
should proceed to Washington, I will on receipt of Instructions to that
effect at once avail myself of the opportunity to impart such
information as it may be in my power so to do.

Very Respectfully 
Your Obt Servt 
John G. Willis
US Coml Agent

Hon Wm. H Seward
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
