Department of State Received June 16 1881 No 72 United States Consulate at St Paul de Loanda to the Department of State Mr Robert S. Newton Abstract of Contents Requesting two new House Flags No 72 United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda 31 March 1881 Sir, I have the honor to request of the Department the favor to send to this Consulate Two full-sized Flag's, as these at present in use are quite worn out and cannot be repaired further. I have the honor to be Your most Obedt Servant Robt S Newton Vice Consul To the Assistant Secretary of State Washington D.C.
