No. 22.19 Department of State Received Feb 27 1888 United States Consulate St. Paul de Loanda December 30th 1887 Mr Downing To the Department of State Subject: Acknowledging receipt of Circular announcing death of Hon. E. B. Washburne. Abstract of contents Same as above. No. 22 19 United States Consulate St. Paul de Loanda. December 30th, 1887 Honorable George L. Rives. Assistant Secretary of State. Washington, D.C. Sir: I have the honor to inform the Department that instruction, relative to "public mourning" contained in Circular of Oct. 24th, will be obeyed. The death of the least useful of our citizens causes hearts to grieve, but when such men as the late Honorable E. B. Washburn "pass on," nations mourn. I have the honor to be, Sir: Your obedient servant, Henry F Downing, U.S. Consul
