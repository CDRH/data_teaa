No 4.

United State Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
October 1st 1887.

Hon James D. Porter
Asst Secretary of State
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform the Department 
that I have arrived at my post and 
have this day taken charge of the Consulate.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient servant,
Henry F. Downing
Consul.
