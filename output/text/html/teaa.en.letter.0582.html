No. 26 Department of State Received Aug 1 1892 Consulate of the United States, Loanda, June 22, 1892. Mr. Heli Chatelain To the Department of State. Subject: Transmitting Report on Angola Abstract of Contents. Filed for publication: a very fine report! No. 26 Consulate of the United States, Loanda, June 22, 1892. Honorable Wm. F. Wharton Assistant Secretary of State, Washington, D.C. Sir: Having often had occasion to notice how little the Province of Angola is known in Europe, and still less in America, and realizing what benefits may result to this Province and to commerce in general if it be presented to the public in a condensed description, I have tried to fill the gap by giving in few words a general idea of my Consular District. I have the honor to transmit to the Department enclosed, a Report on the subject. It is not the reproduction of anything of the kind previously published in any language, but the result of eight years' hard study on the ground. As soon as possible, I intend to draw a map of the Province, also entirely original, which will greatly facilitate the understanding of the Report, and which I hope the Department will publish with it. I have the honor to be, Sir, Your obedient servant Heli Chatelain U.S. Coml Agent Enclosure: 1 Report entitled "Brief Description of Angola"
