Received Jan 28 1867 Legation of Liberia, Yonkers, N.Y. Jany 11th, 1867 Sir I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your note of the 19th instant informing me that my request for the aid of one of the cruisers of the United States, on the coast of Africa for the Government of Liberia would received the earliest consideration; and also that of 21st instant, stating that a copy of my note has been transmitted, for the information of Rear Admiral Goldsborough, commanding the European Squadron, within whose limits Liberia is situated; and I desire to tender herein thanks for the prompt attention accorded. I have the honor to be, with the highest consideration, Sir, your most obedient Servant H. M. Schieffelin To Honble William H Seward Secretary of State Washington D.C.
