







No 36

Dept of State Jul 11 1870

Consulate of the United States
Bathurst, 3rd June 1870

Sir,

Referring to my letter of the 5th
May Ultimo on the subject of
the cession of the Colony by the 
British Government to that of France
the arrangements have not it would 
appear been as yet finally
concluded.

The British Troops have 
been all withdrawn.

I have the honor to be 
Sir
Your most Obedt Servant 
Thomas Brown
Consul

To The Honble
Hamilton Fish
Secretary of State
Washington
