No. 1 Commercial Agency, U.S.A. Boma, Congo Free State Jany 27th, 18892 (now at #1609 35th st. Washington, D.C. Honorable William F. Wharton Assistant Secretary of State, Washington, D.C. Sir: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your despatch No. 1 dated January 22d, and beg to say that I accept the appointment as Commercial Agent of the United States at Boma in the Congo Free State. I enclose herewith Bond and Oath of Office duly executed. I am, Sir Very respectfully R. Dorsey Mohun Commercial Agent Congo Free State P.S. I was was born in the District of Columbia and was appointed from the same place. R. Dorsey Mohun
