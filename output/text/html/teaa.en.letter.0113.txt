







Department of State Received May 14 1878
[No 9]

United States Consulate at
St Paul de Loanda March 1878

Mr Joseph E. Jackson.
To the Department of State

Subject. Renominating Robert
Scott Newton as Vice-Consul

Abstract of Contents

Robert Scott Newton renominated
on March 1, 1878 as Vice-Consul







[No 9]

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda March 1. 1878

Hon F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir, I have the honor to inform
the Department that in accordance
with despatch No 4 of my
instructions, I hereby renominate
Robert Scott Newton as Vice 
Consul at this Consulate.

I am Sir,
Your obedient Servant
Joseph E Jackson.
U.S. Consul
