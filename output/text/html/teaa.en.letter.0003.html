Recd 30th Septr. Salem Mass. Sept 27 1854 Hon. Wm L Marcy Secretary of State U.S.A Sir Circumstances having caused me to leave Loanda previous to giving you notice thereof, I avail myself of the first opportunity since my arrival to inform you that I have left the office of Commercial Agent in charge of my Partner in Business at that place Mr Aug. A. Silva. I am now about closing an engagement to return & when completed intend visiting Washington with some Introductory Letters relating to my appointment as Consul at Loanda instead of Coml Agent. Very Respectfully Your Obt Servt John G. Willis
