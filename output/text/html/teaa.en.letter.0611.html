Department of State Received Dec 3 1883 United States Consulate St. Paul de Loanda 15th October 1883 Sir I have the honor to transmit to the department a translation of the supplement to the Official Newspaper of this Colony, also copy of a circular received from his Excellency the Governor General of the Province notifying that they have assumed in the name of the Portuguese Government the Protectorate of the Country between Landana and Massabe, both included. This is a little to the North of the disputed territory which is causing so much discussion between England and Portugal at present. A fleet of American Whalers and the Schooner "Lottie Beard" of New Bedford are lying in this port at present. Consul Du Verge is still up the Congo river. I have the honor to be Your Obedient Servant Robt S. Newton U.S. Vice Consul To The Assistant Secretary of State Washington. Translation of Official Supplement No. 414 Having come to my knowledge the highly distinguished manner in which the Commander of the Corvette "Rainha da Portugal", Captain Guilherme Augusto de Brito Capello, of the Royal Navy, has executed the Commission entrusted to him to effect the peaceful occupation of a vast portion of the territory north of the paralell 5" 12' South latitude, and placing this brilliant act of the Commander of the "Rainha da Portugal by the Side of the men to whom the Province of Angola owes the greatest gratitude for important Services rendered: I must give praise to the said Commander of the Corvette, Rainha da Portugal, for his devotion to Patriotism and the great ability he has displayed in carrying out his mission. The authorities and other persons whom the knowledge of this may concern will take notice and act accordingly. Government Palace, in Loanda 10th October 1883. (Signed) Francisco Joaquim Ferreira d'Amaral Governor General No 415. Having come to my knowledge the disinterested and highly efficacious cooperation given to the Commander of the "Rainha da Portugal by Mr João José Rodrigues Leitãs in the occupation of the new territories placed under the Portuguese protectorate on the north coast, and desiring to manifest to him the appreciation in which his services are held, crowned with a result so brilliant I have to praise the citizen referred to, João José Rodrigues Leitãs, for the important part which he took in this notable event. The authorities & other persons &c. No. 416 Requiring to establish henceforward in the territories of Cacongo and Massabe recently placed under the protection of the Portuguese Government, an authority to Superintend and regulate the relations of natives and foreigners, guaranteeing to the one the rights of Portuguese citizens which to day they are, and to the others the free exercise of their liberties, I have nominated as delegate of the Portuguese Government in the territories referred to the citizen João José Rodrigues Leitãs. The authorities & other persons &c. No. 19 Circular Translation Most Illustrious & Excellent Sir I have the honor to notify to your Excellency, that by a treaty concluded between the Commander of the Corvette "Rainha da Portugal", Guilherme Augusto de Brito Capello, as delegate of the Governor General of Angola, in the name of the Portuguese Government and the princes of Cacongo & Massabe in grand council, the country which belonged to them was by them placed under the protectorate of the Portuguese Government which exercises over the territories occupied, now and for ever, the right of Jurisdiction which is naturally obtained by its effective occupation. I avail myself of this occasion to once more assure Your Excellency that the most strict Status quo will be maintained to the South of the paralell of 5° 12' S in accordance with the determination of the Government of His Majesty until further resolution. The delegate of the Portuguese Government in the territories annexed is the most Excellent Senhor João José Rodrigues Leitãs. God guard your Excellency Secretariat of the Government General in Loanda 10th October 1883 To The Illustrious & most Excellent Consul of the United States. (Signed) Fancisco Joaquim Ferreira d'Amaral
