No. 5. 3 Department of State Received Aug 31 1882 approved Sept 9/82 United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda S.W.C. Africa L. de R. du Verge to Departt of State Washington D.C. Subject Nomination of Actg U.S. Vice Consul Wm S. Bent Encloses Despatch of same and his signature No 6 3 United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda June 23d 1882 The Hon J. C. Bancroft Davis Asst Secty of State Washington D.C. Sir I have the honor to state that Mr William Stanley Bent was appointed by me this date Acting Vice United States Consul, until the return of United States Vice Consul Robert S. Newton, so that in case of my death the Seals, archives &a. of the United States would be in proper hands I have the honor to be Sir Your obedient servant L. de R. du Verge U.S. Consul. Wm S Bent Signa of Wm S. Bent Actg Vice U.S. Consul L. de R. du Verge U.S. Consul
