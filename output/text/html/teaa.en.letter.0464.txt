







No 10

Department of State,
Washington, August 30, 1890

Emory H. Taunt, Esquire
Commercial Agent of the United
States, Boma

Sir:

Mr. George W. Williams, in
a letter addressed to the 
Department on June 11th,
from Stanley Pool informs
the Department that a 
United States flag is desired
for the "Henry Reed," a Steamer 
belonging to the American 
Baptist Missionary
Union. If the vessel in
question is duly registered
at



at your Agency as American 
owned you can present
her with the eight foot
flag which will be forwarded 
to you.

I am, Sir,
your obedient Servant,
William F Wharton 
Acting Secretary.
