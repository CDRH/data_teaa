Consulate of the United States Loanda, May 30, 1891 I, Edward Bannister, Vice Consul of the United States at St. Paul de Loanda, do hereby certify that, on this thirtieth day of May, A.D. 1891, at the Church of the Carne in the city of Loanda, Frank Weston aged thirty two years, born in Richmond, Virginia, USA and now residing in Loanda, and Aurelia Augusta d'Olviro aged sixteen years, born in Loanda and now residing in Loanda, were united in marriage before me, and in my presence, by Conego Luiz Maria de Carvalho, who is authorized by the laws of Portugal to perform such a ceremony. In witness whereof I have hereunto subscribe my name and affixed the seal of the Consulate at St Paul de Loanda, this thirtieth day of May, A.D. 188 , and of the Independence of the United States the 115th Edward Bannister Vice Consul. L.S.
