No. 21

Consulate of the United States, 
Loanda, September 9, 1890.

Honorable W. F. Wharton
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge
the receipt of despatch No. 37 dated
July 17, 1890, informing me that
I have been appointed United 
States Vice Consul at St. Paul de 
Loanda.

I beg respectfully to tender my
thanks for the honor, and for the
confidence thus placed in me, and
to assure the Department that I shall
at all times endeavor to keep in view
the interests and sustain the dignity
of the nation I am thus called upon
to represent.

I enclose the bond duly executed.
I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant
Ed Bannister
Vice Consul
