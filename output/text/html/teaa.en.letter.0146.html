No 6 5 Department of State Received Aug 31 1882 United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda S.W.C. Africa L. de R. du Verge to Dept of State Washington D.C. Subject Request for blanks Forms, Flags, Boat, Flag Staff &a Encloses. Statement of the conditions in which the Consular archives &a are. No 10 5 United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda June 29th 1882. The Hon. J. C. Bancroft Davis Asst. Secretary of State Washington D.C. Sir I have the honor most respectfully to request that the U.S. Govt. may furnish this consulate with a boat oars and sail. My reasons for asking such are: that a boat here is the most essential piece of furniture this Consulate could have, as to travel by land to the places under this jurisdiction, in a Hammock cost too much, and being a seafearing man, I will be able to manage a boat to any point of my Consular supervision with more speed and less expenses, when such is required for the benefits of Americans and the govt I have the honor to represent here. I have the honor to be Sir Your obedient servant L. de R. du Verge U.S. Consul
