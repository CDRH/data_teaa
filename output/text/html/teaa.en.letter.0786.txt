







Department of State Received Jul 27 1885

St Paul de Loanda
13 June 1885

Sir

Mr Robert Scott Newton,
United States Vice Consul, having
requested that I take charge of
the Archives of the Consulate
during his temporary absence in 
Europe, I have the honour to 
inform the Department that I 
have this day entered upon my
duties of Acting Vice Consul.

I have the honour to be,
Sir,
Your obedient Servant,
Wm S Bent

To the Assistant Secretary of State
Washington
D.C.
