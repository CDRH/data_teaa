Department of State Received Jul 3 1888 U.S. Consulate, St. Paul de Loanda, Washington, July 3, 1888. Mr. Downing To the Department of State Subject Extension of leave. Abstract of contracts Same as above. U.S. Consulate, St. Paul de Loanda Washington, July 3, 1888 Hon. G. L. Rives, Assistant Sec'ty of State, Washington, D.C. Sir: I have the honor to inform the Department that, finding it impossible to dispose of business I have on hand, within the time allowed me, according to the terms of dispatch granting me leave, I would respectfully request an extension of same for thirty (30) days. I have the honor to be Sir Your obedient servant Henry F. Downing Consul
