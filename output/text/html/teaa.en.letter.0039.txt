







Recd Jan 2
No 11

United States Commercial Agency at
Loanda 30th September 1862

Sir

Herewith I enclose Returns
from this Commercial Agency for the
Quarter ending 30th September 1862

The several Accounts and
Certificates I have directed to the
Treasury Department and have drawn
on The Honorable Secretary of the
Treasury for $234. 52/100 Dollars being
balance of my Salary for that quarter

Very respectfully
Your Obedient Servant
Robert Cunningham
Actg U.S. Coml Agt.


To the Honble
W. H. Seward
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
U.S. America
