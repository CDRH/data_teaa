Ackd Feb. 16. '81 Department of State Received Nov 29 1880 No 58 United States Consulate ... at St Paul de Loanda To the Department of State Mr. Robert Scott Newton Subject: Ordering two House Flags: No 58 United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda 14 September 1880 Sir I have the honor to request that the Department will give orders for two House Flags to be sent to this Consulate as those at present in use are in a very bad state. I have the honor to be, Your most obedient servant, Robt S Newton U.S. Vice-Consul To the Assistant Secretary of State Washington U.S.
