







Department of State Received May 25 1886

United States Consulate
St. Paul de Loanda
2nd February 1886.

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge
receipt of circular of November 25th 1885
bringing the sad tidings of the decease of
Vice President Thomas A. Hendricks, and
I beg to tender to the Department and
nation my sincere regret &amp; sympathy for
the loss of so able and eminent public
servant and officer, and to advise the
Department, that according to instructions 
the usual symbols of mourning were assumed
at this Consulate during the period of thirty days

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your most obdt. Servt.
Wm S Bent
Acting Vice Consul

To the 
Secretary of State
Washington 
DC.
