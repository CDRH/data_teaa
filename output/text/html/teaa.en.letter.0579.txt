No. 24

Consulate of the United States,
at Loanda, March 31, 1892.

Honorable Wm F. Wharton
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have to acknowledge the receipt
of your circulars dated Dec. 10, 30, 31
1891, the contents of which were duly
noted.

I have also received a box containing:

(1) A U.S. flag

(2) One Despatch Book and one Record
Book

(3) Some forms and envelopes

(4) Some twine

I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant
Heli Chatelain
U.S. Coml Agent
