Recd Sept 25 No 7 United States Commercial Agency at Loanda 30th June 1862 Sir Herewith I enclose Returns from this Commercial Agency for the Quarter ending 30th June 1862. The several Accounts and Certificates I have directed to the Treasury Department and have drawn on the Honble Secretary of the Treasury for $231. 46/100 being balance of my Salary for that Quarter very respectfully Your Obdt Servant Robert Cunningham Acting U.S. Coml Agt The Honble W. H. Seward Secretary of State Washington D.C. USA
