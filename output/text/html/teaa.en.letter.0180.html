Recd 18 May Legation of the Republic of Liberia New York, 17th, May 1864. Honorable William H. Seward, Secretary of State, U.S. Washington. Sir: I have the honor to acquaint you that, is virtue of powers invested in me by the Republic of Liberia. I have appointed H. M. Scheiffelin, Vice Consul of that Republic for the City and Port of New York, and to request his recognition in that character. I am, Sir, Your obedient servant, J. B. Pinney
