Department of State Received Mar 2 1885 United States Consulate Loanda. January 15th 1885 Sir I have the honor to inform the Department that I have today taken passage for the sailor, Albert Svenson to St Thiago per ss "Portugal." This man is a deserter from the Bark "Horace Scudder" having stolen a boat and gone on shore at Novo Redondo, he was handed over to me by the Portuguese Government having been brought to Loanda, by the Gun vessel "Vilhena" together with another man also a deserter. I put them in prison, but this man falling ill had to go to the hospital and was unable to leave in the ship For his passage I have passed a draft on the Secretary of State @ 15 d/s in favor of Snr Ernst George, Agent at Lisbon of the Steamers for $36 94/100. I have the honor to be Sir Your obedient Servant Robt S. Newton Vice Consul To the Assistant Secretary of State Washington DC
