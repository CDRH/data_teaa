







Department of State Received Apr 28 1888
No. 33.

U.S. Consulate
St. Paul de Loanda,
March 14th, 1888

Mr. Downing
To the Department of State

Subject:

Box of stationery, etc.

Abstract of Contents:

Referring to above.



No. 33.

U.S. Consulate,
St. Paul de Loanda
March 14, 1888

Hon. G.L. Rives,
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to
inform the Department, that
I am in receipt of a case
containing these flags, one
letter press book, one dispatch
book, one miscellaneous record
book, number of Forms and
a lot of stationary. Having received 
no letter of advice I cannot,
as yet, verify contents.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient servant,
Henry F. Downing
U.S. Consul
