







Department of State Received Jun 6 1883

N 48

United States Consulate
At St Paul de Loanda
S.W.C. of Africa

Ls. de R. du Verge
to State Department
Washington D.C.

Subject

Samples of Goods &amp;a having
ready market here.

Encloses

Letter from Henry Pease Esqre
U.S. Consul of Santiago 



United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
April 10th 1883.

Hon John Davis
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge
the receipt of your despatches Nos 13th &amp; 14th
referring to despatch No. 14th, I have the 
honor to state that the Samples of
almost all kinds of goods having
ready market here, were sent by
me to merchants friends in Mass:
New York, Rhode Island and Baltimore
but never heard from them since;
by the same mail I forwarded to
the State Dept same kind of samples
I forward by this mail "China" another
bundle of samples with the hope of
better success.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
Ls de R. du Verge
US Consul
