Department of State Received Apr 2 1888 No. 30. United States Consulate St. Paul de Loanda, February 14th, 1888 Mr. Downing, To the Department of State. Subject: Acknowledgement of Dispatch. Abstract of contents: Same as above, cost of erection and purchase of flag staff of moving and preservation of archives No. 30. United States Consulate, St. Paul de Loanda February 14th, 1888. Hon. G. L. Rives, Assistant Secretary of State, Washington, D.C. Sir: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of despatch number 32, dated Dec. 20. 1887. And, in accordance with instructions contained therein, I have to inform you that the purchase and erection of a flag staff at this Consulate will cost from $110.00/100 to $130.00/100. To some the experience of removing archives I have expanded $8.00/100. What "their proper preservation" will cost it is impossible for me to state. I have the honor to be, Sir, Your obedient servant, Henry F. Downing, U.S. Consul
