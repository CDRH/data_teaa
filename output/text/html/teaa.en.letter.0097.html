Sir, I am sorry to have to inform the department of the death of Commercial Agent, Mr Augustus Archer Silva who died of dropsy on the 22nd June on board the S.S. "Benguella" being at the time on his way to Lisbon. I have the honor to be Sir, Your most obedient Servant Robt S. Newton Vice Commercial Agent To the Honble Secretary of State Washington, U.S. of America
