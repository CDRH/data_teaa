No 17

U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone Feb. 9th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to ask for leave of absence for the purpose of visiting my home in the United States. I desire to leave here in the month May.

I will then have been in Sierra Leone 21 months and find it necessary to go away to recruit & shall return again in the fall. Capt M. W. Tobey the newly appointed Vice Consul will be here and can attend to the bussiness of the Consulate during my absence.

I hope during my sojourn in the states to visit Washington and pay my respects to the Asst Secty of State

I am Sir Your
Obdt Srvt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
