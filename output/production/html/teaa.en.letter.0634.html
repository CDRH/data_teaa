Acknowledgement of Despatch No 5. Nomination of Vice Consul.

 
No 4

U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
Dec 31st 1878

Hon F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your Despatch No 5 under date 12th November 1878, and have Compiled with instructions therein contained, I beg to inform the Department that the two seamen, Henry Miller & Charles Thompson, mentioned in my Despatch No 3 under date Oct 12th, were sent to the to the United States after there discharge from the Colonial hospital.

Henry Miller in the English Brig "Viton" via   via Liverpool on the 9th Oct free of charge.

Charles Thompson in the American Brig Annie Elizabeth of Boston Dec 21st, and for which I gave T. J. Burgess Master, a certificate for Ten dollars to be paid at the Treasury Department after the arrival of the seaman in the United States.

Being informed by Mr. Frame U.S. Vice Consul of his having resigned that post, I therefore nominate Mr Robert Hamilburge to the Department to be appointed Vice Consul at Sierra Leone
