No 32. U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
Judson A. Lewis To Dept. of State

Transmitting Quarterly Returns for Quarter Ending June 30th

 

No. 32. U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone Oct 6th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to forward herewith to the Department the Returns for Quarter Ending June 30th which the Vice Consul neglected to forward during my absence on leave.

I am Sir Your
Obt Servant
Judson A. Lewis U.S. Consul
