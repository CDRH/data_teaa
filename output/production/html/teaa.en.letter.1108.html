Department of State Received Sep 17 1889

No. 28.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, August 5th 1889

Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Receipt of Circulars.

 

No. 28.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, August 5th 1889.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of your Circulars of April 27th, June 8th, 10th, 22nd and 27th 1889. All of which reached this office on the 2nd inst.

Information, in compliance with the instruction contained in the last mentioned, is now being gathered, which will be transmitted as early as possible.

 

I have the honor to be,
sir,
Your obedient Servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith
U.S. Consul General.
