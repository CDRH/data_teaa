Department of State Received Nov 4 1879

No. 34

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, October 1, 1879.

Mr. J. H. Smyth to the Assistant Secretary of State.

Subject:

Quarterly returns.

Synopsis:

The said returns in duplicate, & salary account for 1st Q'r, 1879.

 

No. 34.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, October 1, 1879.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit respectfully herewith the quarterly returns duplicated, for the 1st Quarter, 1879–from July 1st 1879, to September 30th 1879, as follows:

Digest of the Invoice book and transcript of Invoice book, Nos. 1 & 2; dispatches received at legation and consulate general, No. 3; dispatches sent from legation and consulate general, No. 4; arrival and departure of American vessels, No. 5; salary account, No. 6; contingent expenses–abstract & vouchers, (A.); rent and miscellaneous expenses–abstract and vouchers, (B.)

With considerations of distinguished regard,
I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant,
Jno. H. Smyth,
U.S. Consul General.

Inclosures Hon. F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State,
