Department of State Received Jul 7 1892

Consular Series.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, May 26, 1892.

William D. McCoy,
To Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State.

Subject–

Re-nomination of Consular officers.

 

No. 3.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, May 26, 1892.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

In accordance with paragraph 109 of the Consular Regulations, I have the honor to recommend the continuance in office of the following persons:

1. Beverly Y. Payne as Vice Consul-General at Monrovia.

2. G. E. Emminsang as Consular Agent at Elmira.

Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy
Minister-Resident and Consul-General.
