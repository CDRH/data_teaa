Department of State Received Mar 30 1889
No. 15. Consulate General, U. S. A.
Monrovia, Liberia, Feb'y. 18th 1889 Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith.
To the Department of State. SUBJECT:

Report of the Consular Agent at Elmina, Gold Coast Colony, for the quarter ended Dec. 31st 1888.

Monrovia, Liberia, Feb'y 18th 1889 Honorable G. L. Rives,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D. C. Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the report of the Consular Agent at Elmina, Gold Coast Colony, for the quarter ended, Dec. 31 1888, as follows: Arrivals and departures of American vessels; Detailed report of official service to American vessels and seamen; Summary of business; Return of trade during the year 1888.

I have the honor to be, sir, your obedient servant.
Ezekiel E. Smith
U.S. Consul General.

Inclosures, 4.

1. Arrivals and departures of Am. vessels.
2. Detailed report of official service to Am. vessels.
3. Summary of business.
4. Return of trade during the year 1888.
