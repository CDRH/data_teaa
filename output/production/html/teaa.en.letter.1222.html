Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, October 1, 1894.

Mr. Beverly Y. Payne,
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Accounts and returns for the quarter ending September 30, 1894.

 

No 43.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, October 1, 1894.

Honorable Edwin F. Uhl.
Assistant Secretary of State.
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit herewith the Accounts and returns of this Consulate General, for the quarter ended September 30, 1894, as per subjoined list of inclosures.

I have the honor to be,
Sir:
Your obedient servant,
Beverly Y. Payne,
Vice Consul of the United States

Inclosures.

1. Arrivals and departures of Am. vessels.   2. Record of Notarial Services
3. Contingent Expense account
4. General quarterly account
5. Certificate as to absence
6. Vouchers (5)
7. Account Loss by Exchange and Vouchers (2)
