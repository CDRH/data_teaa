Subject:

Abstract of Contents

Instruction No. 17, and inclosure received. Application made for permission for Mr. Williams to act as Commercial Agent of the United States at Grand Bassa. Will report result of application

 

No. 39.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, March 21. 1878.

Sir:

I have had the honor to receive your instruction numbered seventeen, acknowledging my despatches, consular series, Nos. 33. to 37. inclusive, and transmitting oat of office to be subscribed to by Henry Augustus Williams, Esqr. whose nomination to be Commercial Agent of the United States at Grand Bassa is approved. The Certificate of the appointment of Mr. Williams has been received at the Legation and application made in the proper quarter for the Government's permission for Mr Williams to act in the official capacity designated. I hope to have the honor in due course to report the result   result of that application.

I am,
Sir:
With expressions of high esteem,
Your obedient servant
J. Milton Turner
