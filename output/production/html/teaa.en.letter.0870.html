No. 43. U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone Dec. 31st 1880

Hon John Hay
Ass't Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to forward herewith Quarterly and Annual Returns for Quarter and Year Ending Dec. 31st 1880.

I have drawn a Draft on you No. 7. favor Miss Carril E. Hazelton for $19.08 being full amount of Rent & Miscellaneous Expense a/c for Quarters ending June 30th ($2.00) Sept 30th ($1.68) and Dec. 31st ($15.40).

I have also drawn a Draft on you No. 8. favor of Yates and Porterfield for $233.04 being the amount in full - of Account Current with Vouchers sent this day to the Fifth Auditor of the Treasury Department, both of which Drafts I trust will be duly honored

I am Sir
Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul

Inclosures.

Forms Nos 117. 118. 119. 120. 121. 122. 123. 105. 90. with Voucher No 2.
