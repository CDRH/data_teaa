Recd 30th Novr
No. 5

Salem Mass 27th Nov. 1854

Sir,

Yours of the 20th inst. was duly received together with my Passport & Navy Register. I have also received two packages containing the "Statutes at Large" & an American flag, but have not received the Circular of Octo 8th "filled out", nor the Army Register.

I have this day drawn on you at three days after date in favor of Adams & Co for Fifteen dollars being amount of Stationary for the Commercial Agency at St. Paul de Loanda.

I am Sir, Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant
John G. Willis
Coml Agent U.S.A.

Honorable Wm L Marcy.
Secretary of State of the United States
Washington D.C.
