Department of State Received Jan 6 1891

5

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, November 4th 1890.

Mr Beverly Y. Payne,
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Transmitting Quarterly Returns from Elmina.

 

5

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, November 4th 1890.

Hon., William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith, the accounts and returns of the Agent at Elmina, for the quarter ending September 30th 1890, viz, one (1) copy of Arrivals and departures of American vessels, one (1) copy of Detailed Report of Official service to American vessels and seamen, one (1) copy of Summary of business, one (1) copy of Invoice book   I have again to regret that I could not transmit these returns earlier, they were unavoidably miscarried to the windward and have just arrived at this Consulate.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient servant.
Beverly Y. Payne,
U.S. Vice Consul Genl.

Enclosures

1 Arrivals & Departures
2 Detailed Report of Of' Service
3 Summary of business
4 Copy of Invoice Book
