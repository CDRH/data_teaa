Department of State Received Apr 20 1888

Franklinton
Franklin Co. N.C.
April 19th 1888.

Hon. G. L. Rives
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

Not many weeks ago I wrote you in regard to the disallowance the United States Government made of $411.88 which was due me, on my salary for service rendered as U.S. Vice Consul Genl at Monrovia. Please see that justice be given me, for the disallowance is not just.

Hoping my case will receive your attention with full justice. I am, Sir, Â  Respectfully Yours
Samuel S. Sevier
