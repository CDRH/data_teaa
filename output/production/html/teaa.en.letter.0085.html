No 50

United States Coml Agency
St Paul de Loanda Sept 30tg 1868

Sir

I have the honor to inform the department that there were no arrivals or departures of American vessels of this port during the quarter ending this day in requiring no fees received. A sworn statement I forward by this opportunity to the Fifth Auditor of the Treasury.

My acc't current shows a balance in my favor of $250.00 for which acc't I have drawn on this Honable Secretray of the Treasury.

I have the honor
to be
Your Most Obed't Serv't
Augustus Archer Silva
Commercial Agent

To the
Hon Secretary of State of State
Washington
