No 45

Consulate of the United States of America at Zanzibar June 30th 1888

To the
Honorable G. L. Rives
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to inform the Department, that the exchange of the Treaty between the United States and His Highness Kalifa bin Said, Sultan of Zanzibar, took place on the 29th of the present month, between the hours of 10 and 11 in the forenoon, and in usual form.

Respectfully inform also, that I am about sending the exchanged Treaty, and the original protocols of exchange, signed by the Sultans Secretary and myself to the Department by registered mail and through the United States Legation at London, as per instructions received.

I Am, Sir
Your obedient servant
Augustus E. B. Govea
Vice Consul of the U.S. acting Consul
