U.S. Commercial Agency
Monrovia February 22, 1864

To the Honorable F. W. Seward,
Assistant Secretary of State,
United States of America;

Sir;

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your Despatch No 9, of the 18th of September 1863, requesting in formation in regard to the claim of B. V. R. James, as Vice Commercial Agent

I have the honor to inform you that the said James had charge of the duties of the office, as Deputy Vice Commercial Agent, during the "Leave of Absence" of my predecessor, Rev John Seys, in 1862, and that he has been duly compensated for said service, by the said Seys, according to agreement

I have the honor to be
With very sincere Respect
Your Obedient Servant
Abraham Hanson
