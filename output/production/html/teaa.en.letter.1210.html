Monrovia, Liberia, February 28, 1894. Honorable Edwin F. Uhl.

Washington, D. C. Sir:

I beg most respectfully to advise the Department that I have in accordance with instruction, shipped the remains of Minister McCoy, to the American Consul at Liverpool, England, for transhipment to New York, U.S.A: and herewith enclose the Account and voucher for expenses incurred.

I have the honor to be
Sir:
Your obedient servant,
Beverly Y. Payne,
U.S. Vice Consul.

Enclosure
Account and voucher (5) referred to above.
