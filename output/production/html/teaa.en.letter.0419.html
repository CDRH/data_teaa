Department of State Received Sep 12 1871

New York City
September 9th 1871

Sir

I have the honor to report my arrival in this city from Sierra Leone West Africa on the 29th ultmo.

My present address in Care of George S. Gerrard Esq. #34 West 24th St. N.Y.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obt. Servt.
J B Upton
U.S. Vice Coml Agent.

Honorable Hamilton Fish
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
