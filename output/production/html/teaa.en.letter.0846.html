Departure from Sierra Leone and arrival in New York.

No# 34 Smith St
Brooklyn N.Y.
May 17, 1880

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir,

I have to inform you that I left Sierra Leone March 31st and arrived in New York May 6th 1880.

Your Dispatch of May 14th enclosing one copy of Leave of Absence & instructions has been duly received said instructions were duly compiled with & Mr Foley Vice Consul is now in charge of the Consulate at Sierra Leone.

With renewed respect I am very Truly
Judson A. Lewis
US Consul
Sierra Leone
