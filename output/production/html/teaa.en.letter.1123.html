Honorable William F. Wharton,

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to state that in any requisition for supplies of stationery etc for this Legation and Consulate General, under date of October 26th 1889, I failed to request that a supply of despatch paper be included, which request I now beg to make, and hope that a supply of official despatch paper will be forwarded.

I am, sir,
Your obedient servant
Ezekiel E. Smith
U.S. Consul General
