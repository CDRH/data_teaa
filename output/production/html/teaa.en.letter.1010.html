Department of State Received Jan 6 1887

No. 39.

Consulate General of the U.S.
Monrovia Dec. 6, 1886

Mr. Samuel S. Sevier,
To the Secy. of State
Washington D.C.

Subject

Session of the Legislature of the Republic of Liberia.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform the Department of State that the Legislature of the Republic of Liberia met in session Dec. 6, 1886 at the capitol Monrovia according to the constitution of the Republic.
