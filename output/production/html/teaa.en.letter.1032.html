Department of State Received Mar 21 1887

Wyandotte Kansas
March 17th 1887

Hon Jas. D. Porter
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir: On the 16th day of March I sent through the mails my official Bond, which I hope may prove satisfactory to the Secretary of State. The gentlemen who are on my Bond are reputed to jointly represent more than one million dollars. My official oath as you are doubtless aware is on file in the Department of State. My Bondsmen have bee The Senator of our district adds his certificate to the Bond. Your most Obedient Servant
Charles H. J. Taylor

To Hon Jas D. Porter
Asst Sect. of State
Washington D.C.
