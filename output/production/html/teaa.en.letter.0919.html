No. 6

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia 14th August 1875

Sir/

I have had the honor to receive your No. 2. transmitting for execution the bond of Gabriel David Moore, the nomination of whom to be Vice-Consul General of the United States at Monrovia, the Department has seen fit, kindly, to confirm. I have now the honor to inform you with reference to said bond that I have caused the proper execution thereof and return the same herewith to the Department. Your despatch No. 1. has not yet reached the Legation, and Consulate General.

I have the honor to be
Sir/
With high considerations
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner

Honorable John L. Cadwalader
Asst. Secretary of State, Washington
