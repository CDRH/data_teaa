Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of the supplies requested in my despatch No. 36 of the 26th of October, last; and, also, the despatch paper, requested in my despatch, No 42, of the 11th of November last. All of which were received per S.S. "Benin" on yesterday.

I have the honor to be,
sir,
your obedient servant
Ezekiel E. Smith
U.S. Consul General.
