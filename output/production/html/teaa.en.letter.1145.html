Department of State Received Nov 1 1890

No 61.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, September 19th 1890.

Mr Beverly Y. Payne,
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Accounts and returns.

Abstract of contents:

Transmitting account and returns for quarter ended June 30th 1890.

 

No 61.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, September 19th 1890.

Honorable William F. Wharton
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have been informed by Mr. E. E. Smith, that his official function as United States Minister and Consul General to Liberia would cease on the 20th ult., and requested to transmit to the Department of State, his account, receipts and vouchers for the quarter ended June 30th last.

I have the honor to inclose Bond of Treasury Fees, Statement of Account and Vouchers (3) and regret not being able to transmit   transmit herewith complete Returns for said quarter, as Mr Smith was in charge the quarter period of the quarter.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient Servant.
Beverly Y. Payne,
U.S. Vice Consul General.
