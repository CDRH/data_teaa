No: 9
Received Department of State Dec11 1869

Commercial Agency of the United States of America at Sierra Leone 5th November 1869.

Sir,

The United States Flag of this Office being completely destroyed by the rainy weather I request the Department of State will be pleased to furnish me with a couple of new ones.

The custom at this place is to hoist the National Flags daily and the inclemency of the rainy season destroys them rapidly.

I have the honor to be, Sir,
Your obedient Servant
Rosenbush
U.S. Vice Comml Agt

W. B. Davies Esqre
Acting Secretary
Washington D.C.
