Consulate of the United States of America

Freetown, Sierra Leone
July 26th, 1866.

Sir,

I have the honor to inform you, that Mr. Henry Rider, the Consular Agent for the United States at this place, left by the June Mail for England, leaving me as acting in his stead, with the consent of His Excellency Governor Saml W. Blackall.

As The Honorable
The Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs

Sir,
Your obedient Servant
