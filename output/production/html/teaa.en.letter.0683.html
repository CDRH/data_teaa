U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
April 23rd 1879

Hon F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit herewith inclosed, Mr Hamilburges bond Executed. Mr Hamilburge did not return from the river until the 9th inst. and on the next day Executed his bond.

I am sorry to say that owing to pressure of business I omited sending the bond per last steamer.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your Obedient Servant
John A. Parm
U.S. Consul
