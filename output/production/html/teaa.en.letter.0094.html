Sir

I have the honor to inform the Department that Lieut. Grandy's Congo Livingstone Expedition was at St. Salvador Congo River, on June 10th in good health and that they were about to continue their journey inland. They complain that the King of the Congo has detained them at St. Salvador for an unnecessary length of time.

The Portuguese mail streamer "Bengo" from Lisbon landed a few days since at Cabinda a German Expedition on a scientific mission to explore the interior of Africa and the sources of the Rivers Congo and Zambesi.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most obedient Servant
Augustus Archer Silva
Commercial Agent

To the
Honble Secretary of State
Washington
U.S. of A.
