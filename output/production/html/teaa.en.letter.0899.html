U.S. Commercial Agency
Monrovia, Sept. 30, 1862

Hon. W. H. Seward, Secretary of State,
of the U.S. of America, Washington, D.C.

Sir,

I have the honor to enclose herein, a certified statement of Arrivals & Departures of American Vessels, at this Port, for the quarter ending Sep. 20th, 1862.

I also enclose a copy of Recapitulation of the same as found upon the Records.

I have the honor to be, with very great Respect,

Your very obedient servant,
Abm Hanson,
U.S. Com. Agent
