Department of State Received Sep 2 1876

Consulate of the United States
Sierra Leone Aug 3rd 76.

Sir

Agreeably with your despatch No 11 and dated June 26th last I now have pleasure in enclosing you a statement of the Fees received by This consulate during the year 1875.

I am Sir
Your obedient Servant
James France
Acting Consul

John L Cadwalader Esquire
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington
