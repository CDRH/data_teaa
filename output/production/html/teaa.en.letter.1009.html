Mr. Samuel S. Sevier–
To the Secy. of State

Subject.

Returns of contingent expenses of the late Minister Moses A. Hopkins from April 1st 1886 up to date of his death Aug. 3rd, 1886.

Sir:

I have the honor to forward herewith the Contingent expenses of the late Minister Moses A. Hopkins from date April 1st 1886 up to date of his death Aug. 3rd 1886 as required in your   your despatch No. 15 of Oct. 25, 1886. Returns as follows (1) Contingent expenses with vouchers. Hoping the same to be correct.

I am Sir
Your obedient servant
Samuel S. Sevier
