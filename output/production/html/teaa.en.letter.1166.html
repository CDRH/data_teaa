Hon., William F. Wharton,

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the Accounts and returns of the Consular Agent at Elmina for the quarter ended June 30th 1891, as per subjoined list of enclosures.

I beg to state that the mail facilities prevented my transmitting them earlier.

I have the honor to be,
Sir:
Your obedient Servant,
Beverly Y. Payne,
U.S. Vice Consul General

Enclosure.

1. Arrivals and Departures of Am. Vessels.
2. Summary of business.
3. Detailed Report of Official service to American vessels and seaman.
