Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your dispatch Numbers 5 and dated Dec 8th 1879. and note contents of same.

The box containing blanks referred to have been safely recd. Of 41 different numbers required for only 13 are sent. No U.S. drafts. One Flag received

I am Sir
Your obt Servant
Judson A Lewis
U.S. Consul
