Department of State Received July 1 1882
No 95

United States Consulate
at St Paul de Loanda

Mr Robt Scott Newton
Vice-Consul
to the Department of State

Subject.

Acknowledgement of Despatches No 87, 88, and of that dated March 16th the later notifying the appointment of Consul de Verge

No 95

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
13th May 1882

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of Despatches No. 87 & 88, and of Despatch dated Match 16th, the latter notifying the appointment of Consul du Verge to this Port, to whom I will hand over the property of the Consulate, on his arrival, as requested.

I have the honor to be, Sir
Your obedient servant
Robt S Newton
U.S. Vice-Consul

To the
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
