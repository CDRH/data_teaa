Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of despatch number 32, dated Dec. 20. 1887. And, in accordance with instructions contained therein, I have to inform you that the purchase and erection of a flag staff at this Consulate will cost from $110.00/100 to $130.00/100.

To some the experience of removing archives I have expanded $8.00/100. What "their proper preservation" will cost it is impossible for me to state.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient servant,
Henry F. Downing,
U.S. Consul
