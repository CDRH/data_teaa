Department of State Received Apr 9 1887

No. 59.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia March 9/87.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Sec'y of State.

Subject.

Grand Bassa and Cape Palmas.

Synopsis

Consular agents needed at Grand Bassa and Cape Palmas.

 

No. 59.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia March 9/87.

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Sec'y of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that no Consular Agents are at Grand Bassa and Cape Palmas the two important places of trade in this Republic. The late Minster Moses A. Hopkins made no appointments at the above named ports. If it is the wish of your honor, I will go to those places and   and make appointments.

I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant
Sam'l S Sevier
U.S. Consul General

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Sec'y of State
Washington D.C.
