Department of State Received Dec 27 1879

No. 37.

Consulate Gen'l of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, Nov. 14, 1879.

Mr. J. H. Smyth to the Act'g Sec'y, State.

Subject:

Dispatches:

Synopsis:

As to reception by Dep't of several dispatches from this Consulate General.

 

No. 37.

Consulate Gen'l of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, Nov. 14, 1879.

Sir:

Your No. 9, date–Sept. 1st, 1879 acknowledging the reception of my Nos. 19, 20, 21, 22, 23 & 24, consular series; and, also your No. 36, date Sept. 12, 1879 as to receipt of my Nos. 27 to 41 inclusive, same series, received.

I am, Sir,
Your very obd't serv't,
Jno. H. Smyth,
U.S. Consul Gen'l

Hon. Wm Hunter,
Acting Sec'y of State,
Washington, D.C.
