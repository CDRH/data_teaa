Department of State Received Sep 5 1877
No 20

Consulate of the United States of America
Sierra Leone, 27 July 1877

Sir,

Referring to my dispatch No 19, dated 25th inst with reference to three destitute United States seamen forwarded to New York by the American Barque "Jasper" A. M. Miller Master, I have now the honor to report having paid for the lodging and subsistence of the same three seamen the sum of £4.5/" as per receipt herewith enclosed. I have also to report having drawn upon you for the above mentioned sum but in U.S. money at the rate of exchange of [?]4.80 per £ sterling being $20.40 (Twenty dollars and forty cents) at 15 days sight in favor of Messrs Randall & Fischer or their order and commend the said draft to your perfection

Remaining Sir
Your obedt Servant
J Broadhurst
for James Frame
Acting United States Consul

The Honble W. H. Seward
Acting Secretary of State
Washington
