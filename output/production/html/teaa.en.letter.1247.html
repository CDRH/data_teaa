No. 6 Consular Series.
Consulate General of the U. States,
Monrovia Liberia;
Oct. 6, 1898.

Owen L. W. Smith
To
Hon. J. B. Moore,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit to you the Bond of Beverly Y. Payne Esq, Vice Consul of the United States, at Monrovia Liberia. His bondsmen, Hons. Hilary J. Moore, Secretary of the Interior, Republic of Liberia; and Hilary W. Travis, Judge of the Court of Quarter Sessions; I believe to be men of ability, integrity and means Â  means, and amply able to pay the full amount of the bond, should circumstances so demand.

I have the honor to be your obedient servant;
Owen L. W. Smith
