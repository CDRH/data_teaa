No. 7

Commercial Agency of the United States of America at Sierra Leone
6th December 1866

Sir

It become my painful duty to report to the Department the death of William Powers a citizen of Boston Mass, and late master of the bark "Helen Maria" of Salem Mass. I took an inventory of his effects and forward by the "Helen Maria" to his employers an authenticated copy of it.

Before dying he made his will which he left in charge of his [?]

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most ob'dt Servant
Henry Rider
U.S. Com Agent

Hon. W. H. Seward
Secretary of State
