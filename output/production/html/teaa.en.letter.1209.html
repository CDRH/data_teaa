Washington, D. C. Sir:

I have the honor most respectfully to request of you the following supplies for use at this Legation and Consulate General.

1 American Ensign 12 ft length.
1 Small Consular Seal for wax on Consular envelopes.
Stationary for official use, coping enclosures, and miscelleaneous correspondence.
Forms for Reports, Numbers 117, 159, 90, 101 or 102, & 113.
General Quarterly account. Rent miscelleneanus account. Abstract of Contingent Expenses, and Vouchers for Rent.

I have the honor to be,
Sir:
Your obedient servant,
Beverly Y. Payne,
U. S. Vice Consul.
