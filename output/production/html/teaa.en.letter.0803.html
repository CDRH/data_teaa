No 23

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
10th January 1887

Sir

Mr. Robert Scott Newton, United States Vice Consul, leaves for Europe on the 15th proximo and has requested me to assume the duties of the office and take charge of the Archives; I have therefore the honor to inform the Department that on the date above mentioned I shall assume the duties of Acting Vice Consul for the province.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient, humble servant
Ed: Bannister.

To the
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
