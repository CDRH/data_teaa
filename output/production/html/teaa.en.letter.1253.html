Index Bureau Dept of State Rec'd 19 Jan '99

No. 14. Consular Series.
Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia,
Dec. 13, 1898.

Hon. David J. Hill
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit to you the intelligence, that during a very severe electric storm, Saturday night Dec. 10, 1898, lightning struck and so greatly demolished the Senate chamber, and Supreme Court Rooms, that the Senate was forced to assemble, on Monday, Dec. 12, 1898, in Assembly Hall, of West African College.

I have the honor to be your obedient servant; Owen L. W. Smith
