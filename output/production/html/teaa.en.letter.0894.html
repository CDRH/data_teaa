U.S. Consulate, R. Liberia,
August 7th 1862,

Rev John Seys,
Late V.U.S. Com. Agent at Monrovia,

Sir,

Your communication informing me of the appointment of Mr Hanson As U.S. Com. Agent at Monrovia, bearing date 13 May 1862, directing me to hand over to that Gent. on his arrival at the Consulate the archives &c has been accessed also a despach from Sec. Seward to the same effect & purpose all of your command will be cheerfully obeyed when that Gent. arrives. At the same time I hope the Gent. will be willing to pay me for the time of serving since the appointment of Mr Hanson to the time of his arrival at Monrovia.

Herewith I forward over   through you to the State Department the return for the quarter ending June 30th 1862.

Report of arrivals and departures of American vessels–also a report of the Fees received at the Consulate for the said quarter.

I have honor to be
Your most obt. Servt.
B. V. R. James
U.S.V. Com. Agent
