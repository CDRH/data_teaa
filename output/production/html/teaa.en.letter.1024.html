Department of State Received Mar 14 1887

No. 53.

U.S. Consulate General,
Monrovia Jan. 31/87.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Assistant Secty of State.

Subject

Receipt of despatch No. 15. of Dec. 18th/86 with an inclosure from Mrs. E. J. Harmon of Cape Palmas and also a circular.

 

No. 53.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia Jan. 31/87.

Hon. Jas. D. Porter
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

Your despatch No. 15. of Dec. 18/86 with an inclosure from Mrs. E. J. Harmon and also a circular have been received. I shall give my prompt attention to your required instructions. Hoping health and strength be yours during the year you   you have entered

I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant
Saml S. Sevier
Acting U.S. Consul Gen.
