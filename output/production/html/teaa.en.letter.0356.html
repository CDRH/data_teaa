United States Consulate
Zanzibar, November 8th, 1890

Hon. Wm F. Wharton
Assist. Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honour to forward herewith, a copy of the notification of the establishment of a British Protectorate over Zanzibar, received from the British Agency on the 6th inst.

The Protectorate was publicly proclaimed here yesterday. No details are given. Under the present conditions, I consider this Protectorate the best thing that could happen for the interests of the Sultan and all foreigners here.

I have the honour to be, Sir
Your obedient servant
E. D. Roper Jr
U.S. Consul
