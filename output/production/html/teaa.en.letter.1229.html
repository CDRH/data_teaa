No 6.

Consulate Series

Legation of the United States
Monrovia Liberia July 9th 1895

Hon. William Lochren
Commissioner of Pensions
Washington D.C.

Sir:–

I have the honor in replying to the letter sent J. C. Taylor of Arthington Montserrado County Liberia. I find recorded in this Consulate the following

To the Honorable Commissioner of Pension
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I herewith transmit the Documents relating to   the application for Pension by Messrs Bryant Askie, Julius C. Taylor and William Newel. (J. C. Taylor is Julius C. Taylor referred above.)

Signed Beverly Y. Payne
Vice Con. General

Monrovia Liberia Feby' 20, 1894

Mr. Taylor was examined filled out all the papers as did Mr Bryant Askie.

Please inform me if the papers were received?

I am obediently
William H. Heard
Min. Res. and Con. General

Enclosure 1 Letter to J. C. Taylor from Commissioner of Pension
