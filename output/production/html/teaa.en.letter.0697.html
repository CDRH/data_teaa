Department of State Received Jun 2 1879
No 11

John A Parm

To Department of State.

Subject.
Receipt of Stationary.

Abstract of Contents
Acknowledgment of the receipt of Flag, Arms, Stationary & Forms

 
No 11

United States Consulate at
Sierra Leone Africa May 5th/79

To
Hon F.W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of two cases addressed U.S. Consul Sierra Leone. containing Flag, Arms, Stationary and Forms.

I regret that I omited to add to the list of Forms needed. Form No 142. also that there was no Ink sent with the stationary; I will therefore be compelled to have a few of the Forms printed, & Ink purchased at the expence of the Consulate.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your   Your Obedient Servant
John A. Parm
U.S. Consul.
