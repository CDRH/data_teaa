Springfield O. August 20, 1862

Hon. W. H. Seward
Secty of State U.S.

Sir,

I have the honor to inform the Department of State, that the term of my leave of absence from my official post at Liberia being about to expire, and my health being restored, it is my design to return by the first opportunity from New York, early in September.

I desire to be informed whether the President of the United States has any instructions or orders for me in the further pursuance of the duties connected with my office as "U.S. Agent for Liberated Africans."

With the highest respect I have the honor to be

Your most obdt Hlbe Servant
John Seys
U.S. Agent for Lib. Africans
