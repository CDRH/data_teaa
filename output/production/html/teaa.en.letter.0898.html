U.S. Commercial Agency
Monrovia, Sept. 30th. 1862

To the Hon. W. H. Seward, Secretary of State of U.S. America, Washington, D.C.

Sir,

I have the honor to enclose herein my account of Miscellaneous expenses of this Agency since entering upon the duties of my office viz from Sep 4th to Sep 30th inclusive 1862

I enclose the proper vouchers of the parties; one of which is for Labor in trying to remove & repair the old flag staff & procuring a new one.

$3.75

The other is for similar labor as therein set forth.

$4.00

With very great Respect, I am,
Your Obedient Servant,
Abm Hanson
U.S. Com Agent.
