Department of State Received Feb 19 1887

No. 49

Consulate General of the U.S.
Monrovia Jan. 16/87

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Secretary of State

Subject

Returns of the Consular Agency at Elmina Gold Coast for the quarter ending Dec. 31, 1886. Needs of the Consulate.

Abstract Contents

Returns of the Agency at Elmina–needs of Forms, Stationary, Pens &c.

 

No 49

Consulate General of the U.S.
Monrovia Jan. 16/87

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully at this my earliest opportunity to forward you the Consular Agency at Elmina report. The returns, please find consist of Digest of the Invoice Book (1), Arrivals and Departures of American vessels (2) Summary of Consular Business (3)   Report of official services rendered to American vessels and Seamen (4). I have the honor further to inform you that the Agent is in need of printed forms, stationary, pens, pencils, penholders envelops, sealing-wax &c.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
Sam'l S Sevier
Acting U.S. Consul Gen.
