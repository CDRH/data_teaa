No. 22, Consular Series.
Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia.
March 3, 1899.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit to you the intelligence that we have several cases of small pox in the city of Monrovia Liberia. No special effort is being made to prevent its wide spread.

I have the honor to
be your obedient servant;
Owen L. W. Smith,
M. R. and C. General,
To
Hon. David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington.
