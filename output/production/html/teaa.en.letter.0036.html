Recd Sept 25
No 8

United States Commercial Agency
at Loanda 30th June 1862

Sir

I have the honor to inform you that Silas Andrews Esq the newly appointed Commercial Agent has not yet arrived. In consequence of this Gentlemans non-arrival I beg to solicit the appointment as Vice Commercial Agent so as to enable me to be officially acknowledged by the Government of His Most Faithful Majesty, which I have not as yet been.

By the non-appearance here of the Commercial Agent I still hold the office as Acting, it therefore would be very necessary to be informed, whether the above named Gentleman has taken the office The Honble W. H. Seward
Secretary of State
