J Milton Turner

Washington D.C.

Subject:

Acknowldging Consular despatch No. 1. and transmitting summary of fees received during the year 1874.

Inclosures

No. 1. Form No. 105.

 

No. 7.

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia 29th September 1875.

Sir/

I have the honor to receive your No. 1. desiring me to fill consular form No. 105. with which it was accompanied, but to-day; and I hasten to comply with your instruction, and inclose herewith the form filled as requested by you.

I will hereafter endeavor to avoid making the same omission in my December reports. Be pleased to forward to me a few copies of form No. 105.

I have the honor to be,
Sir/
With high considerations and esteem/
Your obedient servant,
J Milton Turner

Honorable
John L. Cadwalader
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington U.S.A.
