Department of State Received Jul 7 1892

No. 4. Consular Series.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, June 4, 1892.

William D. McCoy,
To Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State.

Subject–

Receipt of Circulars.

 

No. 4.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, June 4, 1892.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of circular concerning uniformity in regard to certification of corrected invoices.

Very truly,
Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy
Minister-Resident and Consul-General.
