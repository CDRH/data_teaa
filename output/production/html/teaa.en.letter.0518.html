No 14 United States Consulate
November 28th 1876

Jno L Cadwallader Esqre
Acting Secretary Washington D.C.

Sir,

I have the honor to report for your information that this day William Bishop Seaman called at this Office stating that he had deserted on the 22nd inst: from the Schooner "Nellie Treat" of Bangor

The Schooner cleared at this office for St Thomas West Indies the 21st inst: but did not leave the Port untill last night allowing ample time to notify the desertion at this Office.

I am, Sir
Your Obedient Servant
W H Randall
United States Consul
