Department of State Received Oct 4 1890

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, August 4th, 1890

Mr. B. Y. Payne,
To the Department of State,
Washington, D.C.

Subject:

Elmina Quarterly Returns

 

Hon. William F. Wharton,
Ass't Sec'y of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir.

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith, the Accounts and returns of the Agent at Elmina, for the quarter ending March 31st and June 30th 1890, as follows. Three (3) copies of Arrivals and Departures of American Vessels. Two (2) copies of Summary of Business. One (1) copy of Detailed Report of Official service rendered. One (1) copy Digest of Invoice Book.

I have to regret on account of the late arrival of these Returns Returns   Returns at this Consulate, that I could not forward them earlier.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient servant,
Beverly Y. Payne,
Vice Consul Genl

Inclosures.
Seven (7) as above.
