Dept. of State Recd Dec 29 '68
No 17.

Vice Commercial Agency of the United States of America
Sierra Leone, Novbr: 24th 1868.

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of despatch No 8, dated Octbr: 7th, 1868, informing me of the receipt of my despatches Nos 4, 5, and 12; my oath of Office, which had been placed on the files of the Department, and that a Box containing books, stationary, flag, etc. were sent to the care of the Collector Customs at Boston for remission to me.

I followed a precedent of this Office in numbering all my Official correspondence consecutively Nos A.[?] Hunter Esquire

The American Barque "Helen Maria" of Salem, John B. Hanson Master, entered this port in distress on the 15th instant, having been on Rocks in the Mellacourie River, and has been condemned as being unable to be repaired here. I shall further report to the 5th Auditor.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient Servant,
Rosenbush
