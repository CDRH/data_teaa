United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
April 10th 1883.

Hon John Davis
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your despatches Nos 13th & 14th referring to despatch No. 14th, I have the honor to state that the Samples of almost all kinds of goods having ready market here, were sent by me to merchants friends in Mass: New York, Rhode Island and Baltimore but never heard from them since; by the same mail I forwarded to the State Dept same kind of samples I forward by this mail "China" another bundle of samples with the hope of better success.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
Ls de R. du Verge
US Consul
