Sir:

I have the honor to inform the Department, that I am in receipt of a case containing these flags, one letter press book, one dispatch book, one miscellaneous record book, number of Forms and a lot of stationary. Having received no letter of advice I cannot, as yet, verify contents.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient servant,
Henry F. Downing
U.S. Consul
