Department of State Received Aug 1 1892

No. 5. Consular Series.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, June 21, 1892.

William D. McCoy–
To the Assistant Secretary of State.

Subject–

Submission of a copy of President Cheeseman's Address to the Liberian Merchants.

Enclosure–

1. Copy of President Cheeseman's Address.

 

No. 5.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, June 21, 1892.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to submit to you a copy of an address delivered by President Cheeseman to the Merchants of this city.

Very truly–
Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy,
Minister-Resident and Consul-General.

Enclosure
Copy of President Cheeseman's Address.

 
Enclosure 1/5.

Address of President Cheeseman, to the Liberian and Foreign Merchants, at the Mansion House, May 30, 1892.

Monrovia: T W. Howard, Printer.
