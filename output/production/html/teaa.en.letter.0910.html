No. 3

U.S. Commercial Agency,
Monrovia, June 30, 1863.

To the Hon. W. H. Seward,
Sec. of State, U.S. of A.

Sir,

I have the honor to enclose herein the Quarterly Report of Arrivals and departures of American vessels at this port during the quarter ending June 30th 1863

Also the recapitulation of Navigation and Commerce for the aforesaid quarter.

I have the honor to be, with very sincere Respect,
Your very Obedient Servant
Abraham Hanson,
U.S. Com. Agent.
