No. 25. Consular Series.
Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia,
March 24, 1899.

Owen L. W. Smith
To
Hon. David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit, herewith, a supplemental, statistical report, relative to the Cape Coast Castle Consular Agency. This supplemental report, recovers the last 3 quarters of 1897, including all of 1898 and January of 1899.

In submitting, a partial, statistical report of the Republic of Liberia, in my "Consular," dispatch of Oct. 7, 1898. I referred   referred to the impracticability of giving a full report before the assemblying of the Legislature in December 1898; and promised to send a supplemental report after that date. I have to inform you that we have made several efforts to obtain the desired information, but have in every case been told by the Comptroller, that it could not be furnished, as he was not in possession of same.

I have the honor to be
your obedient servant;
Owen L. W. Smith
M. R. and C. General.
