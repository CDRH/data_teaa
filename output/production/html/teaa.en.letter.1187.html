Department of State Received Aug 18 1892

No. 9. Consular Series.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia July 18, 1892.

William D. McCoy–
To the Assistant Secretary of State.

Subject–

Receipt of box containing supplies.

 

No. 9.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, July 18, 1892.

To the Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor and pleasure of acknowledging the receipt of box containing Record book, blanks, paper, envelopes, and flag, for which please accept the thanks of this office.

Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy,
Minister-Resident and Consul-General
