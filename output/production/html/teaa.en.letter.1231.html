Subject

Julius C. Todd's children

 

No 19

Diplomatic Consular Series

Legation Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia Liberia Sept 14/95

Hon: Alvey A. Adee
Acting Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge yours of the 23rd of July, No 5 containing enclosures from the Secretary of the Mayor of Minneapolis, dated July 16th/95 concerning the estate of Mr. J. C. Todd. The Court has appointed Mr. A. H. Williams Guardian and he has had the care of the children   more than a year. I have put the matter into his hands. He will correspond with the parties holding the property.

I have the honor to be obediently
William H. Heard
Minister Resident and Con. Gen.
