Department of State Received Jan 17 1887

No. 40

Consulate of the United States
Monrovia Dec. 14, '86

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Assistant Secy. of State,

Subject

Despatch No. 14. Message of the President.

Abstract of Contents

Reception of despatch No. 14 dated Nov. 3rd '86. Message of the President of the Republic of Liberia to the Legislature.

 

No. 40.

Consulate of the United States
Monrovia Dec 14, 86

Hon. J. D. Porter–
Assistant Secy. of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor most respectfully to inform you that your despatch No. 14 dated Nov. 3rd 1886 received, I have the honor also to inform the Department that the President of the Republic of Liberia delivered his message today to the Legislature of Liberia in   in joint session.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient Servant
Sam'l S Sevier
Acting Consul Gen.

Hon. J. D. Porter
Assistant Secy of State Washington
D.C.
