Department of State Received Feb 28 1887
No 21.

United States Consulate
at St Paul Loanda
10 January 1887

Mr R. S. Newton
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Advising return to Europe
Enclosing Bond for Ed Bannister

 
No 21

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
10 January 1887

Sir

I have the honor to inform the Department, with reference to my despatch No 16 dated 30 September, 1886, that I purpose leaving for Europe on the 15th of this month and have nominated Mr Edward Bannister to Act as Vice Consul during my absence, and on the 14th shall hand over to him the Archives &c of this office

Enclosed I have the honour to hand you the usual Bond for To the
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.   for the faithful discharge of his duties, in the sum of Two Thousand dollars

I purpose returning by the packet leaving in May next.

My address in London will be
c/o John M Grant Esq
63 Cornhill Chambers
63 Cornhill
London E.C.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient humble servant
Robt S Newton
Vice Consul
