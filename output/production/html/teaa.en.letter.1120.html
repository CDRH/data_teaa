I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the accounts and returns of, Agent of Elmina, for the quarter ended September 30th, 1889, as follows: (1) Arrivals and Departures of American vessels; (2) Detailed report of services; (3) Summary of business.

The same having reached this office on yesterday.

I am, sir,
Your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith,
U.S. Consul General
