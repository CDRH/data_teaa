No 79

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
16th September 1881.

Sir

I have the honor to request the Department to grant me leave of absence for 6 months as both the partners in my Commercial firm having died in April last, it may be absolutely necessary that I should visit Europe about February or March. I will arrange with one of my friends to take charge of the Consulate during my absence, whose name I will advise in good time to the Department. As I only intend going to treat of business matters I do not expect to be away for a longer period than 3 or 4 months.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most Obedient Servant
Robt S. Newton
Vice Consul

To the
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
