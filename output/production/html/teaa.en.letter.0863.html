No. 36. U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
Nov 13th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty State

Sir:

I have the honor to reply to circular letter dated March 19th 1880. regarding the Importation of "Cotton Yarns" as follows.

There are no "cotton yarns" of any kinds imported into Sierra Leone from any Country. No machinery for manufacturing yarn into cloth and no demand for cotton yarn for any purpose

I send under separate cover ten samples of Cotton clothes 1 Cap. and one Cotton Handkerchief with prices attached - all of English make

I am Sir Your obt
Servt Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
