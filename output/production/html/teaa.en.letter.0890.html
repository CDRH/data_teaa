Springfield, Ohio,
May 13, 1862

Hon. W. H. Seward
Sec. of State U.S.

Sir,

Your Dispatch of May 8th has just reached me.

As I know not the address of Mr. Abraham Hanson, I take the liberty to enclose to you an order for the delivery of the Archives of the U.S. Consulate at Monrovia, which you will please deliver, or forward to him.

I have the honor to be most respectfully

Your obt & Humble
Servant
John Seys
late U.S. Vice Com. Agt
