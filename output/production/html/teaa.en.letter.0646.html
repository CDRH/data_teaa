No 6

U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
March 31st 1879

To Hon W. Hunter.
Second Assistant Secretary;
Washington D.C.

Sir.

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your Despatch of the 15th ulto. Nos 7, 8, & 9. Calling my attention to Article X of the Consular Regulation: and enclosing an Official bond to be executed by Mr R. Hamilburge.

Mr Hamilburge is at present out of town, and as soon as he returns I will request him to execute the bond at once, and will forward the same to the Department immediately thereafter.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your Obt Servant
John A. Parm
U.S. Consul
