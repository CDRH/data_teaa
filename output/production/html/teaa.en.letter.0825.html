Amount of Rain Fall in Sierra Leone Comparative Statement showing the "Rain Fall" in inches for each quarter of the years 1877-1878-1879

 

No 6 U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
October 27th 1879

Hon F. W. Seward
Ass't Sect'y of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to forward you herewith a Comparative Statement showing the Rain Fall in Sierra Leone during each quarter of the years 1877, 1878 and 1879

Rain Fall in Sierra Leone

Period Quantity in inches.
1877. 1878. 1879.
Jan. to March 2.02 3.01 5.18
April to June 26.39 32.02 36.88
July to Sept. 70.58 101.74 105.70
Oct. to Dec. 32.61 26.37
Total 131.60 163.14

Free town is so situated as regards [?] and the drains are so well arranged that this immense amount of water is readily conveyed to the sea without doing any damage.

The Health of the Colony during the past rainy season has been remarkably good the Temperature ranging from 76° to 86° in the Shade.

I transmit herewith one Inclosure with tabulated statement of Rain Fall as above.

I am Sir Very Respectfully
Your Obt Servant
J. A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
