U.S. Consulate at Monrovia
Sept. 3d 1862.

Hon. F. W. Seward,
Sec State U.S.

Sir,

I have the honor to enclose to you herewith a true and faithful account of the arrivals and departures of American Vessels at this port (Monrovia) during the quarter to the 3d Sept. 1862. Also account of Treas. Fees received at this Consulate received to date

I have the honor
to be your most obt,
Servant,
B. V. R. James
U.S. V. Com. Agent.
