Judson A. Lewis - To Dept State

Regarding the Announced Blockade of the Brass River - Delta of the Niger, West Coast Africa

No. 34. U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
Oct. 18th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform the Department that the contemplated Blockade of the Brass River - West Coast of Africa - will not now take place - the Chiefs of the Brass River having complied with the demand of Commodore Richards Commanding Her Majesties Ships employed on the Cape of Good Hope and West Coast of Africa Station

I remain
Your Ob't Servt Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
