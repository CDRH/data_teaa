Department of State Received Nov 2 1889

No. 31.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, August 26th 1889

Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Cotton textiles imported into Liberia

 

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, August 26th 1889.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transit, as directed by your circular of May 27, 1889, the within enclosure respecting cotton textiles imported into Liberia.

I have given all information thereon obtainable.

I have the honor to be,
sir,
your obedient servant
Ezekiel E. Smith   U.S. Consul General.
