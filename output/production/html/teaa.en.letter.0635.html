No 5

U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone Dec 31st 1878

Hon F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to transmit herewith inclosed returns and accounts for the quarter ending Dec 31st 1878. I am sorry to inform the Department that I was unable to make out my returns in conformity to the regulations, owing to the want of necessary forms. I therefore beg to call your attention to the following articles which are very much needed at this Consulate viz Flag Flag, Arms, Stationary, Tape, & Forms No 13, 14, 90, 100, 102, 103, 105, 112, 117, 118, 119, 120, 121, 127, 128, 129, 142, 143, 144.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your Obedient Servant
John A. Parm
U.S. Consul
