Department of State Received Nov 13 1888
No. 8 Consulate General, U. S. A.
Monrovia, Liberia, Oct. 1st, 1888 Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith
To the Department of State. SUBJECT:

Returns and accounts for the quarter ended Sept. 30th, 1888.

Monrovia, Liberia, Oct. 1st 1888. Honorable George L. Rives,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D. C. Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to forward the returns and accounts of this Consulate General, as follows: digest of invoice book 1; arrival and departures of American vessels 2; record of treasury fees 3; summary of business for the quarter 4; record of notarial services 5;

I mailed the report, of the Agent at Elmina, Gold Coast Colony, some days since, to you. I have not heard from any other of the Agents in this Consular District.

I am, sir,
Your obedient servant.
Ezekiel E. Smith
U.S. Consul General.
