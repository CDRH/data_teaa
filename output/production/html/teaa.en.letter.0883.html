U.S. Agency for Liberated Africans
Monrovia March 1, 1862

Hon. W. H. Seward
Secty of State U.S.

Sir,

I have the honor to inform you that I have this day drawn in your favor McGill Brothers of this City for Five Hundred Dollars amount due me this day for salary as U. States Agent for Liberated Africans on this Coast.

I have the honor to be with profound respect

Your ost obdt & Humbl Sevt
John Seys
U.S. Agent for Lib. Af.
