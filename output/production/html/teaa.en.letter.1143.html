Department of State Received Aug 29 1890

Goldsboro, N.C. Aug. 25th 1890.

Honorable James G. Blaine,
Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir;

Having been informed, that certain charges or statements respecting my politics have been filed at the Department at Washington; I therefore, most respectfully ask that if such have been filed there that a copy of the same be sent to my address at this place.

I am, sir,
yours very respec't.
E. E. Smith.
