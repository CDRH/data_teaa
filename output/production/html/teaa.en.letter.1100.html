Department of State Received May 11 1889
No. 20 Consulate General, U. S. A.
Monrovia, Liberia, April 1st 1889 Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith
To the Department of State. SUBJECT:

Returns and reports for the quarter ended March 31st 1889.

ABSTRACT OF CONTENTS.   No. 20 Consulate General, U. S. A.
Monrovia, Liberia, April 1st 1889 Honorable George L. Rives
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D. C. Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the returns and reports of this Consulate General for the quarter ended March 31st 1889, as follows:

Salary account 1;
Contingent expenses 2;
Digest of Invoice book;
Arrivals and departures of American vessels 4;
Receipt for rent 5;
Receipt for postal expenses 6.

I hope the same may be found correct.

I am, sir,
Your obedient servant
Ezekiel E. Smith
U.S. Consul Gen'l.

(over)

Inclosures   Inclosures 6.

1. Salary account.
2. Account of Contingent expenses.
3. Digest of Invoice book.
4. Arrivals and departures of Am. vessels.
5. Voucher for rent
6. Voucher for postal expenses.
