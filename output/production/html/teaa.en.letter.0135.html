Department of State Received Dec 14 1881
No 81

United States Consulate St Paul de Loanda

Mr Robert Scott Newton

Subject

Extra wages of W. H. Griffiths discharged from the "Platina"

To the Department of State

 
No 81.

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
30th September 1881

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your Despatch No 81 stating that you enclosed copy of W. H. Griffith's letter which however did not come to hand as there was no enclosure whatever in the Despatch.

In reply I have to state that W. H. Griffiths was duly discharged before me from the Whaler "Platina" of New Bedford, the Master paying into my hands three months extra wages equal to $45:

The expenses incurred with W. H. Griffiths, were the following. viz:

Board & Lodging 24.00
Passage per "Bengo" as per Despatch No 68 24.30
$48.30

so   so that as his expenses amounted to more than his 3 months extra wages, I did not consider that he was entitled to receive anything from this Consulate.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most Obedient Servant
Robt S Newton
Vice Consul

To the
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
