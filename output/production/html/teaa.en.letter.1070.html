Department of State Received Jun 2 1888

No 9

Consulate General of the United States.
Monrovia, Liberia, April 26th 1888.

Mr B. Y. Payne, to the Secretary of State, Washington D.C.

Subject:

Arrival of Governor Rowe at Monrovia, Liberia

Sir.

I have the honor to inform you of the arrival of Sir Samuel Rowe, K. C. M. G. Governor in Chief of Her Britanic Magesty's West African Settlements.

The reception will take place tomorrow 10 O'clock A.M. by His Excellency, The President of Liberia, to which I have an invitation. All information information Â  information obtainable in reference to the cause of his visit, will be communicated to you in due course.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient Servant,
Beverly Y. Payne.

Hon., T. F. Bayard.
Secretary of State
