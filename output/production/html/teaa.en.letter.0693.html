Department of State Received Jan 10 1884

Ranavalomanjaka III, by the Grace of God and the Will of the People, Queen of Madagascar and Defender of the Law of the Country, &c. &c. &c. to our most distinguished Friend, His Excellency the President of the United States of America, &c. &c. &c. sendeth greeting! It is with much pain that we have to announce to your Excellency the most lamented loss we and the Madagascar people experience in the death of Her Majesty Ranavalona II which took place on the 10th Alohotsy (13th July) after a long severe illness. By Her late Majesty's order given out during Her Reign and by the consent of the people we succeed Her as Queen of Madagascar. We assure your Excellency that our earnest desire is to be in good friendship with you and that our subjects and the American Citizens hold a friendly relation thereby to increase Commerce and promote civilization. The Treaties of Peace and Commerce which exist between our respective countries remain unchanged. Given at our Court Masoandro, on the 18th Alohotsy (21st July), in the year of our Lord, and in the first year of our reign

Your good friend
(Signed) Ranavalomanjaka
