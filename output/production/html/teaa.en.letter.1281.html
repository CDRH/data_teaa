Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia;
Jan. 13, 1900.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit, to you the intelligence, that the Hon. Jas. B. Dennis; Secretary of War and Navy; a skilled mechanic, is constructing a Floating Dock; (for the purpose of floating and cleaning the Liberian Gunboats,) for the Liberian Government.

I have the honor to
be your obedient servant;
Owen L. W. Smith,
M. R. and C. General.
To
the Honorable David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington.
