No. 40[?]

United States Consulate

23rd April 1879

Sir,

I have the honor to inform the Department that on account of Mr. Robert Scott Newton's departure for Europe I have this day taken over charge of this consulate together with the archives records & other effects.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most obedient servant
And. G. Colville
Acting Vice Consul

To the Assistant
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
