Recd July[?] 19

United States Commercial Agency at
Loanda 31st March 1862

Sir

Herewith I enclose Returns from this Commercial Agency for the Quarter ending 31st March 1862

The several Accounts and Certificates I have directed to the Treasury Department and have drawn on the Honble Secretary of the Treasury for $232. 87/100 being balance of my Salary for that Quarter.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Servant
Robert Cunningham
Actg U.S. Coml Agt

The Honble
W H Seward
Secretary of State
&c & &
U.S. America
