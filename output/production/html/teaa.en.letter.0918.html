No. 5

Monrovia Liberia July 5th 1875

Sir/

I hasten to apprize you of the inadvertence through which, in my account for salary for the quarter ended June 30th 1875, I omitted to credit the Government for the amount of my draft for one hundred seventy five gold dollars - $125.00/100 - in favor of B. Matthews, and dated May 5th 1875. I will make the proper credit for said draft in my account for the present quarter. In expressing my regret for the above described circumstance, I can only add, that I will, hereafter, endeavor to avoid any similar occurrence.

I have the honor to be,
Sir/
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner

Honorable
John L. Cadawalder
Assistant Secretary of State
