Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the returns and reports of the Consular Agent of the United States, at Elmina, for the quarter ended December 31st, 1889, as follows: Arrivals and departures of American vessels; Summary of business; Digest of Invoice-book; Return of trade with the United States; Detailed report of official services to American vessels.

I am,
sir,
your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith.
U.S. Consul General.
