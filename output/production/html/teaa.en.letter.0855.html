No 28

U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
Sept 15th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Actg Secty State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor respectfully to report myself returned to duty on the 11th Instant.

I left Sierra Leone March 31st

Arrival in New York May 6th

Left New York July 31st Arrival at Sierra Leone Sept 10th

I am Sir Your
Obedient Servant
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
