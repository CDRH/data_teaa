Department of State Received Nov 13 1888
No. 9 Consulate General, U. S. A.
Monrovia, Liberia Oct. 4th, 1888 Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith
To the Department of State. SUBJECT:

Returns of the March quarter from the Agency at Elmina, Gold Coast Colony

ABSTRACT OF CONTENTS. Â  No. 9 Consulate General, U. S. A.
Monrovia, Liberia, October 4th 1888 Honorable George L. Rives
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D. C. Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the March returns of the Agency at Elmina, Gold Coast Colony, which were handed to me on the 2nd inst by my predecessor, Mr. Beverly Y. Payne. I regret exceedingly their long delay. They are as follows: Arrivals and departures of American vessels 1; detailed report of official services to American vessel 2; summary of business 3; digest of invoice book 4.

I am, sir,
Your obedient servant,
Ezek. E. Smith.
U.S. Consul General.
