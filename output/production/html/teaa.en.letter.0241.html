Col. Rooms,
Wash. City, 12 Aug. 1859

My Dear Sir

I have just received a letter from Mr. G. W. S. Hall, of Balt. in which he says, "I have several times been told that Mr. Taylor was born in Balt. His relatives reside here, & several are personally known to me. When I last saw him he had not given up his allegiance, and I have not heard of his doing so since."

Yours most resply W. McLain

G. J. Abbot Esq.
State Dpt.
Washn.
