Index Bureau Dept of State Rec'd Mar 11 1895

500½ South Street
Harrisburg Pa
March 5th 1895

Hon Edwin F. Uhl,
Assistant Secretary,
Honored Sir:–

Your favor of the 28th ultimo has been received, informing me that the President by and with the Consent of the senate had appointed me Minister Resident and Consul General at the United States to Liberia. I gratefully acknowledge the honor conferred, and assure the President that in accepting the position I do so with a[?] sense of the obligation [unclear, one word] in accepting the honor   I will qualify and be prepared to leave in the time prescribed by your instructions.

I am gratefully
William H. Heard
