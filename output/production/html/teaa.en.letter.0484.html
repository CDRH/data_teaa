Department of State Received Nov 8 1875

United States Consulate
Sierra Leone
7th October 1875.

No 3.

Sir,

I have the honor to inform you, this Office has had no Flag since my appointment as Consul and that we have for the past three months been using one belonging to the American Bark "Tropic Bird" and as this vessel haves this port in a day or so the Captain has asked me to return his Flag. Will you please instruct the Department of State to furnish us with a moderate sized Flag or two & oblige.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most Obedient Servant
James Frame
Acting United S. Consul

W. Hunter Esqre
Actg Secretary
