Department of State Received Jun 18 1887

No 71.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia May 17, 1887.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Asst. Secretary of State.

Subject

Receipt of despatch No. 20, under date April 2nd.

 

No. 71.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia May 17, 1887

Hon. Jas. D. Porter
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to inform you that your despatch No. 20. of April 2nd 1887 informing me of the appropriation of $4000, for Consular service at this Consulate is received.

I am, Sir,
your obedient servant
Sam'l S. Sevier
Vice   Vice Consul General of the United States
