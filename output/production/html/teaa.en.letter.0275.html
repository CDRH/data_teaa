Recd Oct 27
No 17.

Consulate of the United States
of America at Sierra Leone
16th September 1863.

Sir

I have the honor to forward you reports for the quarters ending 31st March, and 30th June respectively.

A want of blank forms prevented taking advantage of an earlier opportunity.

For the quarter ending June 30th American goods, to the amount of $75000.00, have been imported in British vessels, which were, formerly, American vessels; the return cargoes of these vessels consisting of African productions amounted to about $70,000.00.

I have to request that a parcel containing the forms required for the verification of Invoices in accordance with the Act of March 3rd 1863 may be supplied to this office.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most ob'd't servant
Henry Rider
U.S. Consul Agent

Hon W. H. Seward
Secretary of State
