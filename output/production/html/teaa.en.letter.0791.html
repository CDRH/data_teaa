Department of State Received Nov 28 1885

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
11th October 1885

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of Circular bringing the sad news of the death of Mr Frelinghuysen; and I beg to tender my regret for the loss of so able an Officer and to advise the Department that according to instructions the usual symbols of mourning were assumed as this Consulate closing the period of ten days.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your most obedient Servant,
Wm S Bent
Acting Vice Consul

To the
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
