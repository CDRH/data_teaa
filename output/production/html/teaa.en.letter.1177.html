Department of State Received Mar 23 1892

No 28

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, February 3, 1892.

Mr Beverly Y. Payne,
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Transmitting Accounts and returns of the Consular Agent at Elmina, for the quarters ended September 30, and December 31, 1891.

 

28

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, February 3, 1892.

Hon., William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith, the Accounts and returns of the Consular Agent at Elmina, for the quarter ended September 30, and December 31, 1891, as per subjoined list of enclosures.

These returns reached this Consulate General yesterday; they were delayed on account of the Consular Agent being without   without certain Forms, which I supplied from this Consulate General in October last.

I have the honor to be,
Sir:
Your obedient Servant,
Beverly Y. Payne,
U.S. Vice Consul.

Enclosure

1 Arrivals and Departure of Am. vessels, Sept'r qr.
2 Summary of Business. Septr qr
3 Arrivals and Departures of Am. vessel Dec'r qr
4 Summary of Business Decr. qr.
