[No 3]

Detroit September 11, 1877.

Hon F. W. Seward,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington D.C.

Sir,

By my instructions, I am directed to proceed to my post at the expiration of thirty days from the date of my oath of office. I find it impossible to arrange my business so soon, and beg the indulgence of the department for two weeks.

I am Sir, your obedient servant,
Joseph E. Jackson,
Consul at St Paul de Loanda.
