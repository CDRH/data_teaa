Receipt of No 7 Consular series

ABSTRACT OF CONTENTS.

Despatch No 7 received,
Receipt for parcel from B. F. Stevens,

Monrovia, Liberia, May 18th 1889 Honorable Alvey A. Adee,
Second Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D. C. Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of your No 7, under date April 2nd, informing me that a supply of the articles requested for the Consular Agent at Elmina, in my Despatch No 16 of Feb'y 28th last had been ordered to be sent to this office.

I received by the same mail which brought your despatch, a receipt from Mr. B. F. Stevens for one parcel, shipped per the steamer Niger, for this Consulate General, but the parcel was not landed by said steamer, which was in port on the 13th inst.

I am, sir,
Your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith,
U.S. Consul Gen'l.
