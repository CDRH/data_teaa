Recd 30th Sept.
No 17

United States Commercial Agency Loanda June 30/61

Sir

Herewith I enclose Returns from this Commercial Agency for the Quarter ending 30th June 1861. The several Accounts and Certificates I have directed to the Treasury Department and have drawn on the Hon. Secretary of the Treasury for $228 36/100 being Balance of my Salary for that Quarter.

My Predecessor John G. Willis last addressed you under date of 29th April informing you of his return to the United States on leave of absence he having appointed me acting commercial Agent at Loanda and being informed of his not returning I beg to solicit the appointment as US Commercial Agent.

I beg to acknowledge the receipt of your Despatch Honble H Seward
