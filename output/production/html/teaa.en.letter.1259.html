Subject

An Official visitation to, and investigation of our Cape Coast Castle, Consular Agency.

 

No. 20, Consular Series.
Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia,
Feb'y 27, 1899.

Hon. David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit to you the intelligence, that according to my Nos. 18 and 19, "Consular," of Jan. 30, 1899, I have made an official visit to, and made a personal investigation of the affairs of our Consular Agency at Cape Coast Castle. My report will accompany or follow this dispatch. I returned to Monrovia, in the afternoon of Feb'y 25, 1899. I have the honor to be   be your obedient servant;
Owen L. W. Smith,
M. R. and C. General.
