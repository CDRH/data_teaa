No. 19

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia, August 8, 1876.

Sir:

I have had the honor this day to receive your despatch No. 6. in which you receipt for my report for the quarter ended March 31, 1876. I have respectfully to request that my unnumbered despatch dated April 21st last, be marked "seperate."

I have the honor to be
Sir:
With high considerations
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner

Honorable
John L. Cadwalader
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington U.S.A.
