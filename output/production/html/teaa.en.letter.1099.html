Acknowledgment of despatch No 5

Monrovia, Liberia, March 28th 1889 Honorable G. L. Rives
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D. C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of your despatch No. 5 of Feb'y 14th 1889, respecting the fees collected by me from Capt. E. T. Page of the bark "Liberia", in Oct. & Dec. last.

I have carefully read article 492 and shall, in future, endeavor to be strictly governed by it.

I have all the while been guided by article 495 Consular Regulations (1888), with reference to collecting fees.

I am, sir,
Your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith,
U.S. Consul General.
