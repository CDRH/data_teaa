U.S. Commercial Agency,
Monrovia, March 31, 1863.

To the Honorable W. H. Seward,
Sec. of State of the U.S. of A.

Sir,

I have the honor to transmit to you herewith the Quarterly Report of Arrivals and departures of American Vessels, at this port, during the quarter ending March 31, 1863.

I have also the honor to enclose the recapitulation of Navigation and Commerce for the aforesaid quarter

I have the honor to be
with very Sincere Respect
Your Very Obedient Servant
Abm Hanson
U.S. Com. Agent
