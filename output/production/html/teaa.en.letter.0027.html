United States Commercial Agency
Loanda 31st December 1861

Sir

Herewith I enclose Returns from this Commercial Agency for the Quarter ending 31st December 1861 The several accounts and Certificates I have directed to the Treasury Department and have drawn on the Honble Secty of the Treasury for $225. 29/100 being balance of my Salary for that Quarter.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Servant
Robert Cunningham
Acting US Coml Agt

The Honble W. H. Seward
Secty of State
D.C.
