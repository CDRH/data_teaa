Department of State Received Dec 2 1889

No. 36

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, October 26th 1889.

Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Requisition for supplies.

 

No. 36.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, October 26th, 1889

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to ask, that the following supplies be forwarded for use, in this Legation and Consulate General. viz: A large United States flag; A dispatch-book; A letter-book; a feebook; An invoice-book; A register for official letters received at the Consulate; A register for official letters sent from the Consulate; A register for landing or debenture certificates; and a   a number of drafts. Also, some pen points and some forms (101).

I have the honor to be,
sir,
Your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith,
U.S. Minister Resident and Consul General.
