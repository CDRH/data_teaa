No. 13

U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone Jan 5th 1880

Hon John Hay
Ass't Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have to acknowledge the receipt at this Consulate of a Book Entitled "Labor in Europe" also the receipt of Circular Letter Dated Nov 22 1879 stating that Mr John Hay has been appointed Assistant Secretary of State and entered upon the duties of his office

I am Sir very Respectfully
Your Obt Srvt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
