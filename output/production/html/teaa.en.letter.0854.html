Brooklyn July 20th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that I sail tomorrow morning at 7 o'clock for my post of duty at Sierra Leone Africa.

I go by sailing vessel direct and expect to arrive in about 30 days from date

I am Sir
Very truly Your
Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
