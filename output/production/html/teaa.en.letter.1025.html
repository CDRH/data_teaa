Department of State Received Mar 14 1887

No. 54.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia Jany 31/87.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Sec'y of State.

Subject

Nomination for the Presidency and Vice Presidency by the Whigh party.

Synopsis

The Whigh party's nominees are Hon. H. R. W. Johnson and J. W. Thompson.

 

No 54.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia Jany 31/87.

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that at the convention of the "Whigh Party" held at Clayashland on the 20th of Jany the present incumbents the Hon. H. R. W. Johnson received nomination for the presidency of the Republic and Hon. J. W. Thompson Vice.   These nominees I am glad to pay him the confidence of the people at large and no doubt success will crown them in the coming campaign. The political feeling of the people is more aroused than usual. I pray health be yours during the year upon which you have entered.

I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant
Sam'l S. Sevier
Acting U.S. Consul Gen.
