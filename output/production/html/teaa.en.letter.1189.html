Department of State Received Aug 25 1892

No. 11.

Consulate-General, U. S. A.,
Monrovia, Liberia, July 20, 1892 Mr
To the Department of State. SUBJECT:

Request for Blanks.

ABSTRACT OF CONTENTS.  

No. 11.

Consulate-General, U. S. A.,
Monrovia, Liberia, July 20, 1892. Honorable
Assistant Secretary of State,
WASHINGTON, D. C.

Sir:

I respectfully request of you the following blanks:

1. Drafts for Salary.
2. Record of Treasury Fees, with oath.
3. Summary of Circular Business.

Please forward by mail immediately as there are none of either of the above on hand.

Very truly–
Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy,
Minister-Resident and Consul-General
