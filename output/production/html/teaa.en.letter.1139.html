Department of State Received May 8 1890

No. 58.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, April 1st 1890.

Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith,
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Returns and accounts for the quarter ended March 31st 1890.

 

No. 58.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, April 1st 1890.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir;

I have the honor, respectfully, to transmit, herewith, the returns and accounts of this Consulate for the quarter ended March 31st 1890, as follows: digest of invoice book; summary of business; arrival and departure of American vessel; record of treasury fees; salary account; contingent account, with vouchers, and an abstract statement.

I am, sir,
your obedient servant
Ezekiel E. Smith.
U.S. Consul General.

List   List of Inclosures:

1. Digest of Invoice Book.
2. Summary of business.
3. Arrival and Departure of American vessel.
4. Record of Treasury Fees.
5. Salary Account.
6. Contingent account with abstract and vouchers.
