Subject:

Transmitting report for the quarter ending the thirty first day of March of this year

 

No. 41.

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia, Liberia, April 1st 1878

Sir

I have the to submit my report for the quarter ending the thirty first day of March of this year. The rooms occupied during the past year for the purposes of the Legation and Consulate General are the property of the American Methodist Episcopal Missionary Society whose agent has informed me that that society requires me to vacate them in favour of a mission school which they intend opening therein. After some search I have been able to secure an eligible location for the Legation and Consulate General to which I have this day removed: hence the increase Honorable Frederick W Seward
Assistant Secretary of State, Washington   increase in my expenditure for contingencies, during the quarter. It is very difficult to procure houses for rent at any price. I regret the increase of Fifty Dollars per Annum in the rent but assure the Department it could not possibly be avoided.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient Servant
J. Milton Turner
