United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
August 14th 1882

To Honoble. J. C. Bancroft Davis
Asst. Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to state that I have just received a letter dated Bailundu S.W. Africa signed Francis O.[?] Nichols secty. of the Am: Mission in which he states that he and all his friends there are well and enjoying good health, also that the country is very beautiful, fertile and healthy, and the natives very kind, beside abundant game.

The Portuguese authorities agree with Mr. Nichols statement, and told me about that country few weeks ago.

I have the honor to be Sir
Respectfully
Your obedient servant
L de R du Verge
US Consul.
