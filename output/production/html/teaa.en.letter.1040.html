Department of State Received May 23 1887

No. 67.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia April 19, 1887.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Secretary of State.

Subject.

Amount due this Consulate General for quarter ending March 31, 1887 as per abstract.

 

No. 67.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia April 19, 1887.

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that an amount of $84.50 is due this Consulate General as per Abstract for the quarter ending March 31, 1887, which for the same I will draw on the "Secretary of State."

I am, Sir,
Your obedient Servant
Saml. S. Sevier
U.S.   U.S. Vice Consul General.

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
