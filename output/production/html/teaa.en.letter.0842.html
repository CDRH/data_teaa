U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
March 27th 1880.

Judson A. Lewis To Dept. State

Regarding Early Departure of J. A. Lewis for the United States.

No 23.

U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone March 27th

Hon John Hay
Ass't Secty State

Sir:

In my Dispatch No 17 of Feb 9th I asked for "Leave of Absence" to enable me to visit the United States. I now find that I am obliged to go sooner than I had contemplated as the vessel is here and will sail on Wednesday March 31st and would most respectfully request that a copy of my leave may be forwarded me at 115 Wall St New York care of Yates and Porterfield On arrival in New York I will immediately Communicate with the Department.

I remain Most Respectfully
Your Obt Servt Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
