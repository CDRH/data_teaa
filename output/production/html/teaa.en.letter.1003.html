Department of State Received Nov 30 1886
No. 32.

U.S. Legation
Monrovia Oct. 16th, '86

Mr. S. S. Sevier
to the Secy. of State

Subject.

Returns for Sept. quarter 1886.

Sir:

After an unavoidable delay I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the returns for the quarter ending Sept. 30th 1886 of this Legation which include that of the late Minister Moses A. Hopkins.

Please find the same   same to be as follows: digest of the Invoice book (1) arrivals and departures of American vessels (2) summary of Consular business (3) record of official fees (4) abstract of contingent expenses with vouchers (5)

I did not receive the fees for this quarter Minister Hopkins did. Hoping this report will meet the approval of the Department State

I am sir
Your obedient
servant
Samuel S. Sevier Vice Consul

 

Enclosures

1. Digest of Invoice book
2 Arrivals & departures of American vessels
3. Summary of consular business
4 Record of official fees
5. Contingent expenses and vouchers.
