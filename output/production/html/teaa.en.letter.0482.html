No 4.

U.S. Consulate
Sierra Leone W.C. Africa
14th September 1875.

Sir,

I beg to inform you that I intend to proceed soon to Madeira for a few weeks residence there.

During my absence I leave in charge of this Office with the Archives &a thereto belonging James Frame Esqre.

The Government here has been duly notified of this fact.
