Department of State Received Jul 11 1870

Commercial Agency of the United States of America at Sierra Leone
11th of June 1870

Sir

I regret that it has become my painful duty to inform the Department of the death of T. A. Rosenbush, late Vice Commercial Agent at this port for the U.S. of America, which took place on the 26th ultmo after a short tho severe illness. Consequently I have resumed the active duties of the Office which he heretofore performed as per communication from him No 8 dated 5th of November 1869 to which I beg to refer you

I have the honor to be,
Sir
Your Obt Servant
Henry Rider
U.S. Coml Agent

Hon. Hamilton Fish
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
