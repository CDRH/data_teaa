Recd 24th Sept
No 11.

Consulate of the United States
of America at Sierra Leone
18th August 1863

Sir,

A case containing forms and stationary for the use of this office arrived on the 14th inst. in the British brig "Hannah" forwarded through the House of Mess E. R. Ware & Co of New York

A despatch, No. 13, dated June 2nd, was received the 11th ult advising of its having been sent

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your ob'd't servant
Henry Rider
U.S. Consular Agent

F. W. Seward Esq
Acting Secretary
