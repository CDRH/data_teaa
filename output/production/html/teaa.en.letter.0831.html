No 12

U S Consulate at
Sierra Leone Dec. 22nd 1879

Hon F W Seward
Ass't Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have to inform you that I sent (per Walter L. Plummer) Four destitute Seamen to New York Dec 18th giving the Capt a Certificate for Forty dollars being ten dollars per Man under the law.

These men L. O Peterson, Chas McElroy, Vernick Lewis and Bennett Peterson from the Brig Julia F. Carney which has burned at Loango.

I am Sir Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U S Consul
