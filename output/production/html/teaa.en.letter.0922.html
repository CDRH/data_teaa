No. 9.

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia Liberia 17th November 1875

Sir:

I have had the honor to receive your despatch No. 3. referring to approval and deposit with the Secretary of the Treasury of the vice-consular bond of Mr David Gabriel Moore; and repeating to me your instruction No. 1. In reply, I have to state that the above instruction was received and honored by me subsequent to my having mailed to the Department my despatch No. 6. in which was mentioned the circumstance of my not having then received your No. 1. But, I have now to inform you that I am without acknowledgment or reply to my Nos. 4. and 5., to the former of which - dated June 30th - it is important that I should receive a reply.

I Honorable
John L. Cadwalader
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington   I have the honor to be
Sir/
With high consideration and esteem,
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner
