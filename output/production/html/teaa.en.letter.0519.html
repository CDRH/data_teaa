Department of State Received Jun 11 1877
No 15.

Sierra Leone
W. Coast of Africa
May 3d 1877

To the Honorable
The Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir,

My health requiring a change of climate, I beg to inform you of my intention to go to Madeira for about a four months' stay.

During my absence James Frame Esqre the duly appointed Vice Consul for this place, will take charge of this Office, its archives &c &c.

Trusting that you will approve of this temporary absence from Office

I have the honor to be
Sir
W H Randall
U.S. Consul
