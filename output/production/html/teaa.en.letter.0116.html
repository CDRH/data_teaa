No. 27.

United States Consulate at St Paul de Loanda
18th September 1878.

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of a Box of Stationary & Flags forwarded from Liverpool, by Consul Fairchild, for the use of this Consulate.

I have to remind the Department the necessity of sending a new Consular Seal of Office now that this post had been made a Consulate. That at present in use bears the impression U.S. Commercial Agency. St Paul de Loanda.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedt. Servt.
Robt S. Newton
Acting Consul.

To The Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
