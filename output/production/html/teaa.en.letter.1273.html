Index Bureau Dept. of State Rec'd Oct 31 '99

No. 35. Consular Series.
Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia;
Sept. 16, 1899.

Hon. David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit to you the intelligence, that we have had a tremendous heavy rain fall, during the present, rainy season. The rivers, it is said, are higher, than they have been for any previous time, in the last ten years. While destitution is great, sickness, calamities and fatalities are not beyond the ordinary, for this season.
