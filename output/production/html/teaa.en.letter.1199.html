Assistant Secretary of State,
WASHINGTON, D. C. Sir:

I respectfully request of you the following supplies for use at this Consulate General:

1 Passport Book.
1 Register of Official Services to Vessels.
1 Relief Book.
1 Protest Book.
1 Daily Journal.
1 Dispatch Book.
1 Consular Seal.
2 Letter Files

If the above are forwarded by or before June 1st, send in my name: if after that time, in the name of Beverly Y. Payne, Vice Consul General

Your obedient Servant,

William D. McCoy
U.S. Consul-General.
