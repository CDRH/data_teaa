Salem Mass
August 14 1861

Sir

Under Date of July 1st, I informed Your Honor that I had returned from St Paul 'd Loanda on Leave of Absence, & at same date sent my Returns for the Quarter ending March 31st 1861.

The Commercial Agency at Loanda is very much in want of a sign & flag. Will you please direct these to be sent to my care at Salem Mass & I will see that they reach their proper destination.

Very Respectfully
Your Obt Servt
John G. Willis
US Coml Agency

Hon Wm H Seward
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
