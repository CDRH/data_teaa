New Richmond State of Ohio, December 7, 1876.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit herewith the accompanying papers making the statement of my account for the first quarter in the present fiscal year. Because of my necessity to crowd many duties into the time granted me for leave of absence, the return of this account has been somewhat delayed; which circumstance is regretted by me.

I have the honor to be
Sir:
With high consideration, and esteem,
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner

Honorable
John L. Cadwalader
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington U.S.A.
