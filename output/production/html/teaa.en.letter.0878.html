Department of State Received Apr 18 1881

No. 51 U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone March 11th

Judson A. Lewis - To Dept of State

Receipt of Department Dispatch No 22. and Circular dated Jan. 25th 1881.

 

No. 51. U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone March 11th 1881.

Hon. John Hay
Ass't. Secty. State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have to acknowledge the receipt at this Consulate by last mail - of your dispatch No 22. dated Feb. 1st and Separate[?] Circular dated Jan 25th 1881.

The former No 22. refered to the American Bark Hazard the Master of which refused to take two destitute American Seamen from this place to Boston. I am glad to know that my course of action regarding said vessel - meets the approval of the Department.

The Circular received refers to the National Board of Health   with revised Fees for Examinations of passengers from foreign ports to the United States.

I am Sir
Most Respectfully,
Your Obt Servt,
Judson A. Lewis
Consul
