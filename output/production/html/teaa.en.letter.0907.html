No. 10.

United States Commercial Agency,
Monrovia, December 31st, 1862.

To the Honorable Wm. H. Seward,
Secretary of State, of U.S. of America,

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt, per the ship "M. C. Sevens," on the 26th inst., of the following circulars & from the Department of State, viz;

Circular No. 17., the instructions contained in which I will promptly carry into effect, as soon as I have the honor to receive a copy of the "Regulations" of the Honorable Secretary of the Treasury therein referred to.

Circulars No. 18., and No. 19.

Circular No. 21. I beg leave to say that I will cheerfully improve every opportunity of forwarding the useful designs of the "American Geographical Society of the State of New York."
