Department of State Received Mar 2 1877
No. 119

United States Commercial Agency
St Paul de Loanda

Mr R. S. Newton
To the Department of State

Subject. Asking for 2 national ensigns.

Abstract of contents.

Advising that the National flags of this Agency are very much worn and requesting new ones. Suggesting sending per Yates & Porterfields ships to Gaboon, thence to Loanda per Steamer.

 
No. 119.

United States Commercial Agency
St. Paul de Loanda 12th Decr 1876

To the Assistant Secretary of State
Washington, D.C.

Sir,

I have the honor to request the Department the favor to send to the Commercial Agency two National flags, of a large size say 15 ft. by 10 ft.

As it is customary here to hoist all consulate flags on Sundays and all public holidays; those now in use at this Agency are considerably worn. A large flag is necessary on account of the height of the flag staff at my residence Messrs   Messrs Yates and Porterfield of New York have often vessels to Gaboon which would be a good way to send them as there are two steamers a month from that port to this

I have the honor to be,
Your most obedient Servant
Robt S. Newton
Vice Commercial Agent
