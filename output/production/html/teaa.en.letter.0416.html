Department of State Received May 25 1871
No 3.

Commercial Agency at Sierra Leone Mch 30th 1871

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of dispatch No 14 dated Febry 21 1871 conferring upon me the appointment of Vice Commercial Agent of the United States at this port - which I hereby have the honor to accept.

The ill effects of the climate on my health compels me to leave soon for America when, with the sanction of the Department I purpose remaining a short time when the necessary bond will be executed

I have the honor to be Sir
Your Obt Servt
J B Upton
U.S. Vice Comml Agt.

To the Honorable
Secretary Of State
Washington D.C.
