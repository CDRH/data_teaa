34 Smith St Brooklyn
July 10th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to Transmit herewith Requisition for Blanks and Supplies for use of Consulate at Sierra Leone if forwarded to me in care of Yates & Porterfield No 115 Wall St New York I will take them with me on vessel free of charges. Please forward soon as vessel is expected to sail Saturday 16th Inst

I am Sir
Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
