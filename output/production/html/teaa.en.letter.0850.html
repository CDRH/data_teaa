34 Smith St
Brooklyn N.Y.
July 3rd 1880.

Hon John Hay
Ass't Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to ask for an extension of my Leave of Absence" for Twenty five days from the 6th Inst. and base my request on the following facts. I received from the Dept of State a Leave for 60 days from May 6th and as there is no vessel sailing for my station before the 20th or the 25th Inst, ask for Extension.

I am Sir
Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul at Sierra Leone
