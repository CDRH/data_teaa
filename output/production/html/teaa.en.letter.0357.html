No. 16.

United States Consulate
Zanzibar, December 5th, 1890

Hon. Wm F. Wharton
Assist. Secretary of State Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honour to inclose for the Department's perusal, a copy of a letter received from the British Agency on the 29th ultimo, and a translation of a letter from the Sultan just received, both notifying this Consulate that hereafter His Highness' foreign relations would be conducted through the British Agency.

I notice that the word "important" before "relations" is omitted in the British Agent's note.

I have the honour to be, Sir,
Your obedient servant,
E. D. Roper Jr
U.S. Consul
