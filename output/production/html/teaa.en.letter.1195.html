Department of State Nov 7 1892
No. 17. Consulate-General, U. S. A.,
Monrovia, Liberia, September 30, 1892. Mr William D. McCoy,
To the Department of State. SUBJECT:

Submission of Quarterly Reports.

ABSTRACT OF CONTENTS.

Inclosures–

1. Summary of Business.
2. Digest of Invoice Book.
3. Arrivals and Departures.
4. Record of Notarial Services.
5. Salary Account.
6. Contingent Expense Account.
7. Abstract of Contingent Expenses.
8. Register of Official Letters sent.
9. Record of Treasury Fees with Oath.
10. Vouchers for Expenditures.

  No. 17. Consulate-General, U. S. A.,
Monrovia, Liberia, September 30, 1892 Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
WASHINGTON, D. C. Sir:

I have the honor of submitting Inclosures numbered from 1 to 10 inclusive, the same being Reports for the Quarter ending this date.

I am, sir–
Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy,
U.S. Minister-Resident and Consul-General.
