Department of State Received Nov 30 1886
No 34.

U.S. Legation
Monrovia Oct. 16th/86

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Assistant Secy. State

Subject

Reception of Diplomatic series Nos 9 and 10. Transit allowance to the widow of the late Minster, granted by order of Department of State. Attention called to complaint against Consular Agent at Elmina.

Synopsis

Diplomatic series, Transit allowance, Complaint against Consular Agent at Elmina (Gold Coast).

 

U.S. Legation
Monrovia Oct. 16,/86.

Hon. James D. Porter
Secy of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to inform you that Diplomatic series Nos 9 and 10 were received and transit allowance in favor of the widow of the late Minister Resident and Consul General Moses A. Hopkins granted.

In regard to the complaint against the Agent   Agent at Elmina Gold Coast of which your enclosure No. 224 of the Vice Consul at Sierra Leone fully states, will receive my immediate attention.

I am Sir
your obedient Servant
Samuel S. Sevier
Vice Consul

Hon. Jos. D. Porter
Assistant Secy. State
Washington D.C.
