C. No. 5

Legation of the United States,
Monrovia, La. Jan'y 7, 1879.

Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge the reception of your No. 14, dated Nov. 12, 1878, on the 4th of Jan'y 1879, and in reply have to state my gratification in consequence of the approval of my conduct in the matter.

I am, Sir,
Your obd't serv't,
Jno. H. Smyth.

Hon. Wm. M. Evarts,
Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.
