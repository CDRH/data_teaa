Department of State Received Mar 18 1887

United States Post Office,
March 16th, 1887.

Hon. Thos. F. Bayard
Sect. of State
Washington D.C.

Sir: I herewith enclose to you my official Bond as U.S. Minister to the Republic of Liberia, subject to your approval.

Very truly, etc.,
C. H. J. Taylor
Wyandotte
Kansas
