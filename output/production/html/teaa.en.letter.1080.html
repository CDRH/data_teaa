No 2.

Consulate Gen'l of the United States,
Monrovia, July 25th 1888

Mr. Ezek. E. Smith to the Assistant Secretary of State.

Subject:

Designating physician to examine Jas. R. Newby

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to inform the Department that, in compliance with the instruction contained in yours of the 8th of June, I have designated Mr. J Henry Roberts M.D., who is the leading physician of Monrovia, to examine Mr. Jas. R. Newby. Dr. Roberts, having been informed of the same, he has signified his willingness to perform said examination. The papers appertaining thereto have been handed him. Mr. Newby has been requested or notified to present himself to said Dr. Roberts for examination.

I have the honor to be, sir,
your obedient servant,
Ezek. E. Smith.
U.S. Consul Gen'l.

To Hon. G. R. Rives
Asst Secty of State.
