Department of State Received November 9 1880

No 33. U.S. Consul at Sierra Leone Oct 7th

Judson A. Lewis - To Dept. State

Forwarding Returns for Quarter Ending Sept 30th 1880.

 

No 33. U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone Oct 7th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst. Sect'y. State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I herewith have the honor to forward you "Quarterly Returns" for Quarter Ending September 30th 1880.

The Vice Consul M. W. Tobey has drawn a Draft on you for $26. No 4 payable to Yates and Poterfield on account of Distressed Seamen during Quarter Ending June 30th. I have also drawn Draft No 5 dated Sept 30th for $77.66 - payable as above on a/c of distressed Seamen during Quarter Ending Sept 30th - both of which drafts I trust will be honored.

I remain Sir
Very Truly Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
US Consul
