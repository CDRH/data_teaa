Department of State Received Jul 23 1873
No 2

United States Consulate
Sierra Leone June 12, 1873

Sir,

I beg to acknowledge the receipt of your dispatch No 4 in which you state that my official Bond from Mr W. F. Exner[?] of New York has been received and has been deposited with the Secretary of the Treasury.

I also have to acknowledge the receipt of my Official passport.

I am
Sir
Your obedient Servant
William Hogan
United States Consul

The Honorable J. C. B. Davis
Assistant Secretary
Department of State
Washington
