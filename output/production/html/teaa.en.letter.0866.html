No 39. U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone Dec. 17th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have to inform you that Despatch No 20 dated November 13th from the Dept of State and saying my despatches numbers 29 to 33 inclusive were received - came duly to hand by last mail.

I am Sir
Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
