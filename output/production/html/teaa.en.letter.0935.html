New Richmond State of Ohio
December 6th 1876

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit herewith, as directed by instruction under date the 16. October, my report for the quarter ended June the 30th 1876, with the necessary corrections made therein. I desire to renew my expressions of regret for the unpresentable state in which that report was at first forwarded to the Department; and to express the hope that the changed condition thereof will be satisfactory to the wishes of the Department

I have the honor to be
Sir:
With high consideration and esteem,
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner

Honorable
John L Cadwalader
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington
