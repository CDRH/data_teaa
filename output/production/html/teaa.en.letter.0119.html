Ward Brothers Ticket Office, 250 N. High St. Columbus, Ohio. Jun 9 1879
Department of State Received Jun 11 1879

Columbus O. June 9th 1879
Assistant Sec. State U.S.A.
Washington D.C.

Dear Sir, Is there any way for us to get even with W. H. Thomas (Colored) a gent who is reported to have been appointed U.S. Consul at St. Paul D Loando Lower Guinea Africa? This man some weeks ago obtained from us under false pretenses a R.R. Ticket Reading Bellaire Ohio to New York via Baltimore and after fair promises &c has skipped to Africa the ticket was worth $11.25. We have plenty of witnesses to the transaction and can give oath to above statement
