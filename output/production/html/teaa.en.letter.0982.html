No. 23.

Consulate Gen'l of the U.S.,
Monrovia, La, July 14, 1879.

Mr. J. H. Smyth to the Ass't Secy, State.

Subject:

Fees.

Synopsis:

As to the annual return of fees received.

 

No. 23.

Consulate Gen'l of the U.S.,
Monrovia, La, July 14, 1879.

Sir:

Your marked "Separate", dated May 24, 1879, received.

I have to say with regard to making an annual return of fees to December 31, 1879, that I cannot comply with your request, which I very much regret; since I find no record of fees kept by my predecessor; therefore, I can furnish only the aggregate amount of fees received by me.

Please find enclosed return of Hon. F. W. Seward,
Ass't Sec'y of State,
Washington, D.C.   of fees from Aug. 15, 1879 - Dec. 31, 1879.

I am, Sir,
With many regrets,
Your obd't serv't,
Jno. H. Smyth,
U.S. Consul Gen'l.

Inclosure:

Aggregate return of fees.
