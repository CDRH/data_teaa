Department of State Received Mar 14 1887

No. 52.

U.S. Consulate General,
Monrovia Jan. 31/87.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Secretary of State.

Subject.

Receipt of despatch No. 17. of Dec. 27th 1886.

 

No. 52.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia Jan. 31/87

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that your despatch No. 17 of Dec. 27, 1886 notifying me of the appropriation of $500.00 for Contingent Expenses of this Legation, has been received. Many thanks for such allowance. Shall the yearly expense for rent come out such   such allowance?

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
Sam'l S. Sevier
Acting Consul of the United States at Monrovia.

To Hon. T. F. Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
