No 9

US Consulate at Sierra Leone Dec. 7th 1879

Hon F. W Seward
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have to inform you of the Death of Revd Albert Bushnell D.D. on the evening of Dec 2nd on board the Steamer Dubriz[?] soon after arrival in this Harbor. Cause of death Bronchitis and congestion of Lungs.

The funeral took place at 4 o'clock on the evening of the 3rd The Flags of this Consulate and of an American Vessel in harbor were displayed at Half mast through out the day.

Very Truly Your Obt Servt
Judson A Lewis
U.S. Consul
