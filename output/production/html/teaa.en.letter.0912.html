No 5

U.S. Commercial Agency
Monrovia Sep. 30, 1863.

To the Hon. W. H. Seward
Sec. of State of U.S. of America

Sir:

I have the honor to enclose herein the Quarterly Report of Arrivals and departures of American vessels at this port during the quarter ending September 30, 1863.

I also enclose herein the recapitulation of Navigation and Commerce for the aforesaid quarter

I have the honor to be
With very Sincere Respect
Your Obedient Servant
Abm Hanson
U.S. Com. Agent
