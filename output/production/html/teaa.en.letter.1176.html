Department of State Received Jan 25 1892

419 North Mississippi Street.

Indianapolis, Indiana,
January 20, 1892.

Honorable James G. Blaine,
Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

In response to yours of the 18th, instant I will say that I have the honor of accepting the position of Minister-Resident and Consul-General to Liberia.

Enclosed please find Oath and Bond as required.

In compliance with your letter, I shall await further instructions before leaving here, but hope to be able to leave on Saturday, January 30th., which will allow one to report to you in person on the following Monday morning.
