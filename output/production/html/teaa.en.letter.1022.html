Department of State Received Mar 1 1887

No 51.

U.S. Consulate
Monrovia Jany 24th/87.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Assistant Secty of State.

Subject

Settlement of the funeral expenses of the late Minister Moses A. Hopkins.

 

No. 51.

U.S. Consulate
Monrovia Jany 24/87

Hon. Jas. D. Porter
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that I have this day settled the funeral expenses of the late Minster Moses A. Hopkins with G. T Hoove & Son: And further I would inform you that I paid Rev. Thomas H. Roberts $50.00 for preaching funeral sermon according to the bill he presented.

 

I transmit herewith account of settlement with vouchers. The urgent demand of G. T Hoove & Son for payment has prevented me from waiting further instructions according to your despatch No. 13. of Sept 30, 1886.

Hoping what I have done will meet the approval of the Department State.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
Saml S Sevier
Acting U.S. Consul Gen.
