No. 44, Consular.
Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia;
Jan. 25, 1900.

Hon. David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington.

Sir:

I have the honor to say, that the present session of the Liberian Legislature, in order, to prevent smuggling; and the better to secure the collection of import duties, has enacted a law, for the erection and establishment of bonded warehouses, in every port of entry.
