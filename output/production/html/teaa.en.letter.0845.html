34 Smith St
Brooklyn New York
May 17th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty of State

Sir

I forward herewith the Quarterly Consular Returns (Sierra Leone Consulate) for Quarter Ending March 31st 1880. Proper returns also forwarded 5th Auditor this day. Not having time to forward the same by Mail from Sierra Leone I brought them with me & forward them from this place.

Trusting all will be satisfactory
I remain Your
Obedient Servant
Judson A. Lewis
U S Consul
