Monrovia, Liberia, September 12, 1892 Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
WASHINGTON, D. C. Sir:

Inclosed please find letter containing a remittance ($5.00) to the New York Herald, for subscription.

A bill was sent to me but as it was not properly receipted I have requested that another one be sent to you, to be kept until I submit my Report for the Quarter, that it may be included in the contingent expenses for this Quarter.

As I have three Daily papers on my personal list I have requested that the Herald be discontinued that I may have the Weekly Tribune and London Times (Weekly) instead.

Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy,
Consul-General
