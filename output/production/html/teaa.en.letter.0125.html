No 58

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
14 September 1880

Sir

I have the honor to request that the Department will give orders for two House Flags to be sent to this Consulate as those at present in use are in a very bad state.

I have the honor to be,
Your most obedient servant,
Robt S Newton
U.S. Vice-Consul

To the
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington U.S.
