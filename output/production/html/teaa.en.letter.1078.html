Department of State Received Aug 13 1888

No 13.

U.S. Consulate,
Monrovia, Liberia,
July 2nd 1888.

Mr B. Y. Payne to the Assistant Secretary of State

Subject:

Returns for the Quarter ended June 30th 1888.

 

Sir,

I have the honor respectfully to transmit the following Returns for the quarter ended June 30th 1888, which consist of Digest of Invoice Book, Arrival and departures of American vessels Summary of Consular business, Records of official fees Miscelleneous expenses with vouchers General, Account current

I beg again to state that I have out no House rent in my account, as that matter has not had any attention of mine, as I have had no advice from you: but Mr. Jesse Sharp has rendered his bill to date, which I enclose enclose   enclose for your information.

I trust these Returns will be found correct, and give satisfaction.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient Servant,
Beverly Y. Payne.

Hon. George L. Rives
Ass't Sec'y State.

Enclosures

2 Arrivals and departures of vessels

4 Record of official fees
