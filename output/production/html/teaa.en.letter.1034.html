Department of State Received May 7 1887

No. 61.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia April 4th/87.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Secy of State.

Subject

Returns for quarter ending March 31, 1887.

Synopsis

Digest Invoice Book. Arrivals and Departures of American vessels. record of official fees. Summary of Consular Business. Abstracts of contingent Expenses Account for salary. Account for cable-gram.

 

No. 61.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia April 4./87

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Secy of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the returns for the quarter ending March 31, 1887 of this Consulate General. Please find the same to be as follows:

Digest of the Invoice Book (1) Arrivals and departures of American vessels (2) Record   Record of official fees (3) Summary of Consular business (4) Abstract of Contingent expenses (5) Account for salary (6) Account for cable-gram (7).

The returns for Gold Coast are not enclosed because up to this time they have not been received.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient servant
Sam'l S. Sevier
U.S. Vice Consul Genl

Inclosures

1. Digest of Invoice Book
