No 55

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
23rd July 1880.

Sir,

I have the honor to inform the Department that, despatch No 75, and circular No 18, and circulars dated Feby 18th and 24th, and March 19th marked separate, have been duly received at this Consulate and will have due attention.

Circular of Feby 24th referring to my not having sent in the weekly reports required by the National Board of Health. The reason of my not having done so is that it is difficult to get correct information, and that since my return to my post I have not been able to attend to it owing to illness, but will see to it in the future.

Circular 19th March, referring to Cotton Yarns. No Yarns of of the quality mentioned are used in this market, but I will get samples of Cotton goods used here and as requested send them with all information as soon as possible.

I have the honor
to be Sir
Your most Obedient
Servant
Robt S Newton
Vice Consul
Acting Consul

To the
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
