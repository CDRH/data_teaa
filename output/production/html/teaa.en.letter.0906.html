U.S. Commercial Agency
Monrovia, Dec 31 1862

To the Hon. William H. Seward,
Sec. of State of U.S. of America,

Sir,

I have the honor to enclose a "Recapitulation" of my Quarterly Report of Arrivals and Departures of vessels, at this Port, ending Dec 31, 1862

And have the honor to remain,
With very Sincere Respect, &c &c
Your Obedient Servant,
Abraham Hanson,
U.S. Com. Agent.
