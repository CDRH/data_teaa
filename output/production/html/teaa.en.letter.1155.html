Department of State Received Feb 26 1891

No. 8 Consulate General Series,
Legation of the United States.
Monrovia Liberia December 31, 1890
Mr Alexander Clark,
To the department of State Washington D.C.

Subject.

Accounts and returns.

Synopsis.

Abstracts of contents:

Transmitting accounts and returns for the quarter ending December 31, 1890

 

No 8 Consulate General Series:
Monrovia, Liberia Dec 31, 1890.

To the Honorable William F. Wharton Assistant Secretary of State Washington D.C.

Sir;

I have the honor to transmit the accounts and returns of this consulate for the quarter ending December 31, 1890 per the Subjoined list of enclosures. And I respectfully call your attention to the account of house rent. I have inclosed two (2) quarters my predecessor Hon Mr. E. E. Smith. having paid only for the quarter ending June 30th 1890. and Vice Consul Payne did not feel authorized to draw for said rent - ending September 30th as you will see per his report. September 30th 1890 Dispatch 67 & & &

Enclosures   Enclosures &.c

1 Digest of Invoice Book
2 Arrivals and Departures of vessels.
3 Record of Treasure Fees.
4. Contingent account abstract[?] and vouchers (10)
5 Sallery Account for the Quarter ending Dec 31 1890

Sir;
I have the honor to be your
Obedient Servant
Alexander Clark.
United States Minister Resident and Consul General at Monrovia
