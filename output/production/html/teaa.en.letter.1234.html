Subject

Supplies and furniture for Legation

 

No 10

Consular Series

Legation of the United States
Monrovia Liberia Feb'y. 10 1896.

Hon. Edwin F, Uhl
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor in replying to your Consular Series No. 9 of December 20, 1895 to say, that I gave Hon. Alvey A. Adee Acting Secretary State on Aug. 20 1895 the estimate asked for by you, with exception of letter files and pigeon holes, in my Diplomatic Series No 17. Hon Richard Olney Secretary of State Authorized me in   his Diplomatic Series No 12 Sept 23 1895. to spend not more than $125.00 for rebinding Archives, repairing and painting furniture, purchasing desk and chair for office. The work has done, the furniture ordered from New York at a cost of $111.50 not including letter files and pigeon holes. I have drawn draft No 27 in favor of Fred'k Grant Monrovia Liberia for the amount.

I am obediently
William H. Heard
Minister-Res. & Con Gen
