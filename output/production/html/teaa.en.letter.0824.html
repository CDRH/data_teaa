No 5

United States Consulate at
Sierra Leone Oct 12th 1879

Hon F W Seward
Ass't Secty State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to forward herewith Requisition for Official Blanks as are now required at this Consulate

Also for two Small Flags such as are required for every day use & for rainey weather - as the only Flag now on hand is a Large one

I would also respectfully acknowledge the Receipt of Circular No 17 regarding a Book entitled "The Wealth of the United States" - and would suggest that Two (2) Copies (English) We sent to this Consulate

I am Sir Very Respectfully
Your Obt Sevt
J. A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
