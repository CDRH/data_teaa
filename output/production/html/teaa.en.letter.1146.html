Washington, D.C.

Sir.

I have the honor respectfully to inquire of you if Congress made appropriation for the insufficiency fund for Consular service of 1888? The holder of my Draft for June quarter of that year informs me that it has not yet been paid.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient Servant,
Beverly Y. Payne
U.S. Vice Consul Genl.
