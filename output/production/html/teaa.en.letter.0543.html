Department of State Received Dec 17 1877

United States Consulate
Sierra Leone 10 Novbr 1877

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your circular dispatch dated 17th September 1877 and in compliance with the instructions therein contained have prepared a report showing the rates of tonnage tax, exacted on the vessels entering the port of Sierra Leone, accompanied by a detailed statement of all the charges on American vessels upon clearance and enhance during the last completed fiscal year. This report I have the honor to forward enclosed and I remain
Sir
Your most obedt Servant
J. Broadhurst
for James Frame
Acting Consul

The Honble F. W. Seward
Acting Secretary of State
