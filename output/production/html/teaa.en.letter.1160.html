3. Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia, Liberia, April 9th 1891.

Hon. William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your No 3 of February 13th with enclosure of Messrs Yates Brothers Shattuck and Company's letter to the Hon. Secretary of State in reference to the deposition of Captain Frank Nelson, taken by the Consular Agent at Cape Coast Castle, and beg most respectfully to say in reply, that I have instructed Consul Agent Eminsang to forward same immediately, and as soon as it is received will be transmitted to you.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient Servant
Alexander Clark
US. Minister & Consul Gen
