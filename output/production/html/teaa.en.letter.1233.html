Monrovia Liberia Dec. 3rd 1895

Hon. Edwin F, Uhl
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have investigated charges against Mr. B. Y. Payne for drunkness and find them unfounded, therefore most respectfully ask his reappointment as Vice Consul. His bond has been newly approved.

I am obediently
William H. Heard
Minister Res and Con. Gen.
