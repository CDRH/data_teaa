Department of State Received Apr 18 1876

United States Consulate
Sierra Leone W.C. of Africa
March 18th 1876

John L. Cadwalader Esqre
Assistant Secretary
Department of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your dispatch No. 7 dated January 29th 1876 and requesting me to transmit Duplicates of my dispatches No 2 and 5. I regret that I am only partially able to comply with your request as through some oversight it has been omitted to number each letter forwarded from this Office. Enclosed I now beg to hand you Duplicate of Dispatch No 2 adding adding that from the communications received in return from your Department, none of my dispatches can have gone astray. In future I shall be particular to have all dispatches duly numbered.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
W H Randall
U.S. Consul
