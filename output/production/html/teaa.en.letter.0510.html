Department of State Received Nov 27 1876

United State Consulate at Sierra Leone
to the Department of State:

No 12

Mr. John L Cadwalader James Frame

Subject. Acknowledgements of Despatches Nos 12 & 13 of date 5th July - with Case Stationary - also certificate of appointment.

Enclosing Good Notes for Prize money Nos 4. 10 & 204 respectively.

 
No 12

United States Consulate
Sierra Leone
Octr 24th 1876.

John L. Cadwalader Esqre
Assistant Secretary
United States

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of Dispatches No 12 and 13 dated July 5th with the case of Stationary as advised.

I have also to acknowledge the receipt of Certificate of my appointment signed by the Honble Secretary of State from the Legation in London.

I have also the honor to call your attention to a claim for prize money on this Consulate made by a Krooman calling himself Tom Walker, the original notes showing holders claim to the share of prize money and numbered respectively 4. 10. & 204. In the event of this claim being invalid from any cause and there is no money due Tom Walker please return the notes to the Consulate

I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant
James Frame
Vice Consul United States

Enclosures
Three Good Notes for Prize Money.

Copy.

No. 4.

I certify that Tom Walker Krooman, was on board the U.S. Ship Constellation, when she captured the Barque Cora and the coast of Africa, 25" Sept. 1860, and is entitled to share in said prize - also in the Brig Delicia, captured 21st Dec. 1859, same Ship.

Jno N. Hambleton,
Pay Masters

Approved
J. S. Nicholas,
Captain.

  J. M. Turner,
U.S. Minister,
Monrovia.
Copy.

No. 10.

I certify that Charlie Mac Krooman, was on board the U. States Ship Constellation when she captured the Barque Cora, on the coast of Africa 25" Sept. 1860, and is entitled to share in said prize - also in the Brig Delicia - captured 21st Decr. 1859 by same ship.

Jno N. Hambleton, Pay Master

Approved;
J. S. Nicholas,
Captain

Copy

No. 204.

I certify that Josiah Anderson, 2d Class Boy was on board the U.S. Ship "Constellation" when she captured the barque "Cora", on the coast of Africa, 25" Sept. 1860, and is entitled to share in said prize. Also in the big "Delicia" captured 21 Decr. 1859 by same vessel.

Jno. N. Hambleton,
Pay Master.

Approved:
J. S. Nicholas
Captain.
