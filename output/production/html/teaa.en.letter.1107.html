Department of State Received Sep 17 1889

No 27

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, August 5th 1889.

Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Accounts and returns of the Consular Agt. at Elmina, for the quarter ended June 30th 1889

 

No. 27.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, August 5th 1889

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to transmit herewith the accounts and returns of the Consular Agent at Elmina, for the quarter ended June 30th 1889, as follows: Arrivals and departures of American vessels; Detailed report of official services; Summary of business.

I have the honor to be, sir,
Your obedient servant
Ezekiel E. Smith
U.S. Consul General.
