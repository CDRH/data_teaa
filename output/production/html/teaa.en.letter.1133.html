Honorable James G. Blaine,
Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of Department Circular of November 11th, 1889, with reference to the laws and regulations on the manufacture and sale of wine, beer and similar fermented liquors.

I have complied with the instructions contained in said Circular, and beg to state that neither wine nor beer of any description is manufactured or fermented in Liberia.

I am,
sir,
your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith.
