No 6 Dispatch Consular Series,
Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia December 13, 1890
Mr Alexander Clark to the Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State, Washington DC

Sir;

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of your printed tariff circular of date October 15th 1890 Instructing me to prepare and forward to the department a report upon the duties on importes and exports as established by the Laws of the Republic of Liberia &c In accordance with said instruction, I am pleased to report that I have carefully compiled a schedule list of the custom duties collected under the present tariff Laws of Liberia, giving a list of export duties and the Free[?] list &c

 

Inclose find Inclosures No one.

Marked Pages No. 1 2 3 4 5 & 6

Alexander Clark
Minister Resident and Consul General of the U.S.
