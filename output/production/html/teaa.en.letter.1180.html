Department of State Received May 10 1892

No. 2. Consular Series.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, April 7, 1892.

William D. McCoy
To Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Subject–

Request for a Record book.

 

No. 2.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, April 7, 1892.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

Permit me to add to my request for supplies (No. I), a Record book for American vessels arriving at and departing from this port.

Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy,
Minister-Resident and Consul-General to Liberia.
