James Spriggs Payne
President of the Republic of Liberia,
To Joseph Jenkins Roberts: Greeting.

Reposing special trust and confidence in your Integrity, Prudence and Ability I do appoint you the said Joseph Jenkins Roberts a citizen of this Republic, Envoy Extraordinary for the Republic of Liberia, to the government of the United States of America; authorizing you hereby to negotiate and form with the said government of the United States of America, a Treaty or Treaties of Alliance, and Commercial Reciprocity; And to do and perform all such matters and things as may be dully given you in charge hereafter; and the said office to hold during the pleasure of the President of Liberia. In testimony whereof I have caused the Seal of the Republic be hereto affixed.

Given under my hand at the city of Monrovia this eighteenth day of June, in the year of Our Lord, one thousand eight hundred and sixty nine and of the Republic of Twenty Second.

James S. Payne

J H Johnson
Secy of State
