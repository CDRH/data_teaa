Department of State Received Nov 15 1877

Consulate of the United States
Sierra Leone 10th Oct 1877

Sir

I have the honor to forward enclosed statement of fees received at this consulate during the year ending 31st of December 1876 and remain Sir

Your obedient Servant
J Broadhurst
for Jame Frame
Acting United States Consul

The honorable F. W. Seward
Acting Secretary of State
Washington

One inclosure "Statement of Fees received during the Year 1876."
