Rec 24 Decr
No 11.

United States Commercial Agency
Loanda Octo 23, 1860.

Sir.

Herewith I enclose Returns from the Commercial Agency for the Quarter ending Sept 30 '60. The General accounts & Certificates I have directed to the Treasury Department, & have drawn on the Hon. Secretary of the Treasury for $189.12Â¢ being balance of my Salary for that Quarter.

I last had the honor of addressing you under date of Sept 15th enclosing Returns for the Quarter ending June 30th 60. Under this date I informed your Honor that a large number of Troops had arrived here from Lisbon. I now have to add that a Fever resembling the Yellow Fever has broken out among them & nearly one hundred have died within the past month. As yet it is mostly confined to the Hospital among the Soldiers, although there have been cases outside. All who have as yet died with this disease have lately arrived here & it may possibly be Ship Fever. I enclose the Bolletin in which you will perceive the Government are endeavoring by cleanliness &c Hon Lewis Cass
Secretary of State
Washington DC &c to prevent the disease from spreading.

This Agency is very, much in want of a new flag,. also Forms Nos 14, 30, & 44. Will you be pleased to direct them to be sent. Vessels belonging to the House of Brookhouse & Co Salem, Mass. leave there every two months for this Port & will forward anything the Department may desire.

Very Respectfully
Your Obt Servt
John G. Willis
US Coml Agent
