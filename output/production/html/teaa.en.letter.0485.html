No. 6.

United States Consulate
December 11th 1875

John L. Cadwallader Esqre
Acting Secretary
Washington D.C.

Sir, I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your despatch No. 5 and in reply beg to report my return from Madeira the 8th inst. In the event of my temporary absence from the Colony in future I nominate as Vice Consul Mr. James Frame or Mr. John Broadhurst both Partners in the firm of Messrs Randall & Fisher. Apologising for having neglected hitherto my instructions in this particular.

I am Sir
Your obedient Servant
W H Randall
Consul
