Department of State Received Jun 11 1877
No 16.

Sierra Leone
W. Coast of Africa
3d May 1877

Sir,

I beg to acknowledge receipt of your Circular dated March 22 - no number - informing me of the appointment of the Honorable W. H. Seward to be Assistant Secretary of State.

Taking due notice of the above communication

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
W H Randall
U.S. Consul

To
The Honorable Evarts
Department of State
Washington D.C.
