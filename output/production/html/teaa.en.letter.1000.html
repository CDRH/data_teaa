Monrovia, Sept. 3rd '86

Mr. S. S. Sevier,

Subject:

The reception of Dispatch No. 8, with the enclosed letter of Lucy C. Calloway, and also the intelligence of the death of Mr. Wm Hunter, the Second Assistant Secretary of State.

Legation of the United States,
Monrovia, Sept 3rd '86

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that Dispatch No. 8, with the enclosed letter of Lucy C. Calloway, and also the intelligence of the death of Mr. Wm Hunter, the Second Assistant Secretary of State, have been received.

The usual symbols of mourning will be displayed at this Legation.

I have the honor, sir, to be
your obedient servant,
S. S. Sevier
Act. Consul General
