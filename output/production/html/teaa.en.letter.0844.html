Official Gazette.

His Excellency the Governor-in-Chief has been pleased to recognize Mr. Marcellus William Tobey provisionally to act as Vice-Consul for the United States in Sierra Leone, pending the receipt of Her Majesty's Exequatur.

By His Excellency's Command,
T. Risely Griffith,
Colonial Secretary & Treasurer.

Colonial Secretariat & Treasury,
Sierra Leone,
20th April, 1880.

Printed by Simeon Hemmings John, Acting Government Printer.
