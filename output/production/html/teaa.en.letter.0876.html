Sir:

I have to acknowledge the receipt at this Consulate of the Register of the Department of State Corrected to Dec. 1880. and under same cover - Circular dated Dec 27th 1880. regarding the detention[?] of Invoices of Merchandise destined for Geneva (Rochester) also Circular dated Jan 7th 1881. Enclosed price List of Coin from the Secretary of the Treasury.

I am Sir
Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U S Consul
