Department of State Received Sep 9 1875
No 1.

U.S. Consulate
Sierra Leone, August 11, 1875

W. Hunter, Esqre
Second Assistant Secretary
Department of State
Washington
USA

Sir,

I had the honour to receive your dispatch No 3 dated June 30, a.c. requesting me to transmit to your Department the statement of the aggregate of fees collected at this place for the year 1874. I regret that I am not able to comply with your request as during the time in question there was no Consular Officer of the U.S. here & consequently no entries whatever were made. For the future I shall not fail to fulfill the prescriptions of the Consular Regulations.
