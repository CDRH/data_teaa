Department of State Received Sept 13 1873
No 4

United States Consulate
Sierra Leone August 4/73

Sir,

I regret to inform you that in consequence of ill health I am compelled to proceed to Europe by first Mail Steamer. My absence will not I hope exceed three months.

I leave in charge of the Consulate during my absence Nathaniel Lyons Esqr and have notified the Government of the fact.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your Obedient Servt
William Hogan
U.S. Consul

The Honble J. C. B. Davis
Assistant Secretary Department of State Washington
