Department of State Received Oct 25 1880

No 30 US Consulate Sierra Leone

Judson A. Lewis To Dept State

To remains of the late Rev A. Bushnell forwarded to Gaboon

Â 

No 30

U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone Sept 27, 1880.

Hon John Hay
Ass't Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have to inform you that I have had the remains of the late Rev. Albert Bushnell (who died at this place Dec. 2nd 1879.) exhumed and forwarded to Gaboon to Mrs Bushnell who is now at Gaboon. This is done in accordance with the request of Mrs Bushnell and the Ladies of Auburn New York. The remains were forwarded per "S.S. Nubia" on Saturday Sept 25th 1880.

I am Sir
Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
