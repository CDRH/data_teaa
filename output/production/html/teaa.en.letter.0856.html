Department of State Received Oct 25 1880

No 29. U.S. Consulate Sierra Leone Sept 25th

Judson A. Lewis To Dept of State

Circular Letter Dept of State July 1st 1880 received

The above circular Letter wherein Consular Officers are inferred that Congress at its last session made provissions for printing and distributing more frequently the publications by the Dept of State of the Consular & other Reports.

No. 29.

U.S. Consulate at Sierra Leone
Sept 25th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have to acknowldge Letter Department of State dated July 1st 1880. wherein Consular officers are informed that Congress at its last session has made provisions for the more frequent publication & distribution of Consular and other reports

I am Sir
Your Obt Servt
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
