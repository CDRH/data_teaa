United States Consulate at Sierra Leone

Mr W. H. Randall

Subject

No 29

United States Consulate
Sierra Leone
11th May 1878

Sir,

My health necessitating a temporary change of climate I beg leave respectfully to notify my intention of leaving the consulate in charge of my partner Mr John Broadhurst in the absence of the Vice Consul Mr James Frame.

Trusting this will as in previous instances meet with the approval of the department of state, and hoping to return and resume my official [unclear, one or two words] in about four months

I have the honor to remain
Sir
Your most obedt servant
W H Randall
U.S. Consul

The Honorable F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State
