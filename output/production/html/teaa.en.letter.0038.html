Recd Sept 9.

Salem Mass Sept 6, 1862

Sir,

Enclosed please find Draft for Twenty one & 7/100 dollars, being balance due me as pr a/c of Treasury Department, which I would respectfully request sent me in Draft on the Sub- Treasury in Boston payable to my order.

I would also request sent me previous to the 18th inst. that being the time I intend leaving this Port for St Paul 'd Loanda for the use of the Commercial Agency the following viz.

Forms Nos 8, 14, 15, 16, 17, 24, 25, 30, 31, 33, 41, 44, 88, 94, 105, 109.

Bills of Exchange or Drafts. Exchange Vouchers Nos 1 & 2. also one Large flag.

Trusting that my communication addressed under date of Sept 1st to Hon F W Seward assistant Secretary of State has received your Kind attention & that I may expect a favorable reply previous to my departure as above

I have the honor to be
Your Obt Servt
John G. Willis.

Hon Wm H Seward
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
