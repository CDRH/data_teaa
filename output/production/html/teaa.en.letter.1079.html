Department of State Received Aug 13 1888

No 3.

U.S. Consulate,
Monrovia, July 10th 1888.

Mr E. E. Smith to the Secretary of State.

Subject

Arrival at Monrovia.

Sir.

I have the honor to inform you of my arrival (this day) at Monrovia. I have further to state that the voyage was 39 days.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient Servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith,
U.S. Minster &c.

Hon.,
T. F. Bayard,
Secy State
