No. 53.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, February 1st 1890.

Honorable Alvey A. Adee,
Second Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir;

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of Department Circulars severally dated, November 25th & 27th and December 6th & 18th 1889.

Also duplicate copies of the Annual Report of the Treasurer of the United States for the year 1889. The Annual Report of the Comptroller of the Currency. The Annual Report of the Fifth Auditor, The Annual Report of the Secretary of the Treasury, Duplicate copies of the Annual Report of the Secretary of the Interior, and copies of the Reports of the Consuls of the United States, to No. 109, October 1889.

I am, sir,
your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith,
U.S. Consul Gen'l.
