United States Consulate
St. Paul de Loanda
Augt. 12th 1882

To
Honorable J.C. Bancroft Davis
Asst. Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor most respectfully to request that blank forms and stationary be forwarded to this consulate as soon as possible besides Eight U.S. Flags; Two for this consulate which is in need of the same, and six other for the Consular Agents on this coast, together with six Consular Regulations; for which this consulate will be responsible.

I would also respectfully aske that another U.S. Coat of arms be forwarded to me, as the old one is now unfit for use

I have the honor to be Sir
Respectfully
Your obedient servant
L de R du Verge
US Consul
