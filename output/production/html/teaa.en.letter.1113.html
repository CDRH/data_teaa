No. 32

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, September 19th 1889.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Acting Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor, respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of Department Circular of July 31st 1889, with reference to Canals. I beg to state in reply thereto, that there is not a canal in Liberia.

Permit me, also, to acknowledge, herein, the receipt of Vol. 25 of the United States Statutes at Large; and Consular Reports to Vol. 105 of May 1889.

I am, sir,
Your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith,
U.S. Consul General.
