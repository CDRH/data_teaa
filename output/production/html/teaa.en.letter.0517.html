Department of State Received Dec 31 1888

St. Paul de Loanda

British Consulate Loanda
15 November 1888.

Sir

It is my painful duty to have to report to the Department the death of Vice Consul Judson, who died here suddenly on the 8th inst. It would appear that Mr. Judson went to the government office to get a passport to go to Cabinda, and was seized with a fit on the stairs. He was immediately put into a maxilla and sent to his house which was close by. Dr. Oliveira was called, and did all he could for him, but he died almost before the medicine could be had from the Chemist. Enclosed you will find certificate of death.

Immediately I heard of the occurrence, I went to the Secretary who is at present acting Governor, and requested him to allow me to take charge, and as my appointment as Vice Consul for the United States has not been officially advised to the Portuguese Government as having been annulled, he said he would not interfere. I therefore took charge of the Funeral The Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C. funeral, inviting only the Consuls of the different Four nations, and authorities. The funeral took place on the following morning at 7 oclock in the English Cemetery the American missionary Mr. McLean reading the Funeral service.

I have taken full charge of all his effects, and of the Archives which I find are in a plate of the utmost confusion, and I am therefore unable to send a proper inventory of the same by this mail, but will do so by first opportunity.

I shall be happy to continue in charge of this Consulate should the Department wish me to do so

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient servant
Robt S. Newton
HB Majesty's Acting Consul

Translation

José Baptista de Oliveira, Physician of the School of Medicine & Surgery of Lisbon &c

I certify that Mr Judson, Consul for the United States of America in this city, died suddenly of Congestion of the lungs so suddenly that I did not have time to give him any treatment.

In witness of the truth of which, I hereby sign
(signed) José Baptista de Oliveira

Loanda 15 November 1888
