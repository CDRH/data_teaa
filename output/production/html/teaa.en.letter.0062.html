London May 10th 1865

Sir

In accordance with my Dispatch to the Department of State dated St Paul de Loanda December 31st 1864 informing you of my unavoidable visit to this country and my reasons for so doing. I have the honor to say I arrived here on the 4th ultimo and on the 20th inst I return to my post of duty upon Steamer [?] via Lisbon.

While at Lisbon on my way here I had the honor of an interview with our minister Mr Harvey and explained to him my views on the necessity of creating a Consulate at St Paul de Loanda instead of a Commercial Agency. I recommend this alteration[?] not with any motive of personal [?] [?] or pecuniary[?] benefit, but only that the representative of the United States should at least rank with Consuls of other nations
