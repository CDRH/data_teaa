Department of State Received May 4 1888
State Colored Normal School,
(Instituted 1877.)
E. E. Smith, A. M., Principal.

Fayetteville, N. C., May 2nd 1888.

To the Hon. T. F. Bayard,
Secretary of State,
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your communication of the 1st inst, accompanying the Bond and "Oath of Allegiance and Office."

I shall proceed, at once, to qualify[?] and return the papers.

I shall report to you in person within[?] six or eight days.

I am most obediently,
Ezekiel E. Smith.
