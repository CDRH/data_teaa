Department of State Received Aug 14 1879

Sierra Leone West Coast of Africa
July 9th 1879

Hon F. W. Seward
Assistant Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I hereby acknowledge the receipt of your communication of June 3rd/enclosing to me blank forms &c regarding my appointment as U.S. Consul as this place.

I hereby accept the said appointment with thanks.

I return to you the "Oath" duly signed and acknowledged to before a justice of the Peace. My Bond is signed by Felix Dalmas and Hon Syble Boyle, both honorable merchants the former Consul to the "King of the Netherlands" the letter a member of the "Executive Council" of this colony.

The present "U.S. Consul" through whom my papers were sent to one - not only opened my letter but retained it 26 hours. in consequence of which I could not return these papers by the mail & they are delayed one week.

Very respectfully Judson A. Lewis
