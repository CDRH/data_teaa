Consulate of the United States
Sierra Leone Feby. 21st 1874

To the Honble J. C. B. Davis
Assistant Secretary
Department of State
Washington U.S.A.

Sir,

I have this day drawn on you, favor of Messrs Mansfield & Roberts of Boston, for the sum of Seventy three dollars ninety two cents, for moneys paid by me, for boarding, lodging, and clothing three destitute American Seaman, as per vouchers enclosed. Be pleased to honor my draft.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most obdt Servant
Nathl Lyons
Acting U.S. Consul for Sierra Leone
