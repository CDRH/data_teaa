Washington, D.C.

Sir:

In compliance with the instructions conveyed in the Circular dated 20 September 1890, I have the honor to transmit herewith a "Report on the production of Crude India Rubber" in the Province of Angola and its dependencies.

The delay in forwarding it is owing to want of quick communication with the sources of information, and certain difficulty in getting at statistics.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient servant
Ed Bannister
Vice Consul.
