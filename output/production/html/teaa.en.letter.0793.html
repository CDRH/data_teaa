Department of State Received Nov 28 1885

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
12th October 1885

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of Despatch No 19 Series 1 of July 18 1885, and of Circulars dated June 29, July 17, & 15

I beg to advise the Department that this Consulate is without a flag to hoist over the Consulate, the one left by Mr du Verge being near[?] in a very dilapidated state, and I have the honor to request that one or even two be sent.

I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your most obedt Servt
Wm S. Bent
Acting Vice Consul

To the
Secretary of State
Washington D.C.
