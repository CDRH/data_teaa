Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to advise, that I have this day drawn Draft No 4, on the Secretary of State for Two Hundred Dollars, in favor of Messrs. Edwards, Brothers, of Liverpool, England, on account my Salary.

I have the honor to be,
Sir:
Your obedient Servant,
Beverly Y. Payne,
Vice Consul Gen.
