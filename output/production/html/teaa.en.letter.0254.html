Recd 11 April

Washington, April 6, 1859

Hon John Appleton
Assistant Sec'y of State

Sir,

This is to certify that I, the undersigned; was at the Gaboon West Africa on the arrival of Henry A. Ford M.D. appointed U.S. Consular Agent for that place, on July 1st 1856, & that he was at that place the whole time to the date of his death Feb 3, 1858, attending to sick & distressed seamen, & performing the duties of Consular Agent. I was in the Gaboon the whole of that period, & was with him at the time of his death.

Very Respectfully Yours
Wm. Walker
Missionary of the A.B.C.F.M.
