No. 140.

Consulate of the United States
Zanzibar, April 4 1890.

Honorable William F Wharton
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your despatches numbers 64 and 65. dated respectively the 1st and 14th February 1890 which have my attention.

With reference to your despatch number 62 of the 10th January - I would say that Dr. B. F. Wilson strongly object to the designation of the Governor of Mayotte as arbitrator in his claim against the King of Johanna. I may say in passing that I concur with Dr. Wilson in his decision in that the Governor of Mayotte has has an indirect interest in the outcome of the case.

Enclosed herein is a copy (in duplicate) of Consular Corrections at [?] for the month of March.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
Seth A. Pratt U.S. Consul.

Enclosure:

Form No. 100.
