U.S. Consulate at
Monrovia March 5, 1862

Hon. W. H. Seward
Scty of State U.S.

Sir,

I have the honor to enclose you herewith a true and faithful account of the Arrivals and Departures of American Vessels at this Consulate during the Quarter from January 1 to this Date.

I have the honor to be Sir,

Your most obdt & Humble Sert.
John Seys
U.S. Vice Com. Agt.
