Department of State Received Nov 17 1890

No 63.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, September 30, 1890.

Mr Beverly Y. Payne,
To the Department of State.

Subject:

Accounts and returns.

Abstract of contents.

Transmitting accounts for the Quarter ended September 30th 1890.

 

No 63.

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia, Liberia, September 30th 1890.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington, D.C.

Sir.

I have the honor to transmit the Accounts and returns of this Consulate for the quarter ended this date, as per subjoined list of inclosure. Salary and rent account will be transmitted at earliest possible date after I shall have heard from the Department in reference to the time which Mr Smith's official functions ceased.

I have   I have the honor to be,
Sir,
Your obedient Servant,
Beverly Y. Payne,
U.S. Vice Consul General.
