Department of State Received May 31 1890

No 59.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, April 29th, 1890.

Mr. Ezekiel E. Smith
To the Department of State.

Subject:

John S. Bowles, Co. D. 5 Ohio vols. Santa Cruz, Bolivia.

 

No 59.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, April 29th 1890.

Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir;

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of your Circular dispatch of March 11th, 1890, with reference to John S. Bowles of Santa Cruz.

I am, by said circular, directed "to designate a competent physician to examine John S. Bowles of Santa Cruz, an applicant for pension under Certificate No. 734543."

As   As Santa Cruz, Bolivia, is quite a distance from this post, and as the instruction contained in the said Circular directs me to incur no expense whatever in attending to the affair save the medical examination fee; and as I am requested to give prompt attention to the Commissioner's request, I beg to return said pension papers of John S. Bowles and ask further instructions relative thereto.

I am, sir,
your obedient servant,
Ezekiel E. Smith
U.S. Consul General.

Inclosures:
Pension Papers of John S. Bowles.
