No. 37

United States Consulate at Zanzibar February 13th 1888

Honorable George L. Rives
Assistant Secretary of State

Sir:

I have the honor to inform the Department of having taken charge of this office, on the 1st of the present month. The new bond will reach the department under separate cover, having been sent to Boston Mass to be executed.

I am Sir:

Your Obedient Servant
Augustus E. B. Govea
U.S. Vice Consul, acting Consul
