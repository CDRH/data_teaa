Department of State Received Aug 11 1892

No. 7. Consular Series.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, July 2, 1892.

William D. McCoy,
To the Assistant Secretary of State.

Subject–

Receipt of Instructions and Enclosures from the Commissioner of Pensions, in reference to a Medical examination of James Bowie of Cape Palmas, Liberia.

 

No. 7.

Consulate of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia, July 2, 1892.

To the Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington, D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that your instructions and enclosures from the Commissioner of Pensions in reference to a medical examination of James Bowie of Cape Palmas, bearing date of May 19, are at hand.

The matter shall receive my immediate attention.

Very respectfully–
Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy,
Minister-Resident and Consul-General
