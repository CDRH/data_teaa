Subject

Renomination of Subordinates

 

No 5.

Consular Series

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia Liberia July 1st '95

Hon Edwin F. Uhl
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to ask Continuance in Office of Mr. George E. Eminsang Consular Agent at (Elmina) Cape Coast Castle.

Mr Beverly Y. Payne Vice Consul General at Monrovia Liberia. The continuance of Mr. Payne as Vice Consul General is conditional, I learn he is not sober. If after careful investigation I find such to be   the truth I will make another nomination. But if I find to the contrary he will continue. Will the State Department allow this matter to remain private until I am satisfied?

I am obediently
William H. Heard
Minister Resident and Con. General
