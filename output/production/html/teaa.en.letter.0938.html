No. 21.

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia, Liberia, March 10. 1877

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit herewith, with expressions of regret at the lateness thereof, my report for the quarter ended December 31, 1876: The detention of this report until this date was owing to my absence in the United States.

I have the honor to be
Sir:
With high considerations
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner

Honorable
John L. Cadwalader
Assistant Secretary of State, Washington.
