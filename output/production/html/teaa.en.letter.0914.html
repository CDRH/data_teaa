No. 1

United States Legation
Republic of Liberia

J Milton Turner
to Department of State
Washington D.C.

Subject:

Relating to the nomination of a person to be appointed Vice Consul

 

No. 1

Legation of the United States
Monrovia 15th April 1875

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of the Department's Despatch marked "seperate" and dated 25th February, inviting my attention to the nomination under the Regulations, of a person to be appointed Vice Consul at my post of duty; I beg to state: that the instruction shall receive my immediate attention so soon as I shall have been able Honorable
John L Cadawalder
Assistant Secretary of State   able to select a suitable person.

Indeed, I should have long since respected, rather observed the requirement of the Regulations in this direction, but for my inability to make my selection of a person adapted, and at the same time available for the position of Vice Consul

I have the honor to be
Sir,
With high consideration, and esteem
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner
