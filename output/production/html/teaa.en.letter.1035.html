Department of State Received May 7 1887

No. 62.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia April 4th/87.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Assistant Sec'y of State.

Subject

Receipt of Despatch No. 17 with inclosure; also Blank forms for drafts.

Synopsis

Receipt of Despatch No. 17– two circulars of instructions and blank forms for drafts.

 

No. 62.

U S Consulate General
Monrovia April 4th/87

Hon. Jas. D. Porter
Assistant Sec'y of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to inform you that your despatch No. 17 with inclosures of two (2) circulars was received. Draft forms also received.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient servant
Sam'l S. Sevier
U.S.   U.S. Vice Consul General
