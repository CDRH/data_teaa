Sir:

I have the honor respectfully, in compliance with the instructions contained in your circular despatch dated August 16th 1889, to forward by this post to the Department, two copies of the National Anthem of Liberia. I could not possibly obtain a copy earlier than this date.

I have the honor to be,
sir, your obedient Serv't,
Ezekiel E. Smith.
U.S. Consul Gen'l.
