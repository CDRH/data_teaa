Department of State May 7 1887

No. 63.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia April 4th/87

Mr S. S. Sevier
To the Secretary of State.

Subject

Receipt of circulars dated Feb'y. 8th and 23rd/87.

 

No. 63.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia April 4th/87

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that your circulars dated Feb'y 8th and 23rd/87 were received.

Your instructions shall be carried out to the pleasure of your honor the Secretary of State.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your obedient servant
Saml S. Sevier
U.S.   U.S. Vice Consul Genl.
