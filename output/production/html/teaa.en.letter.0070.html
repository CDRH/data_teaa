Recd March 23
No 18

United States Coml Agency
St Paul de Loanda January 17th 1866

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge the reception of Your Despatch No 7 and also Communication of October 21 ultimo

I shall expect to receive the Flags & Forms by the first vessel from Salem which will be in about two months

Trade is reviving again The New Palm Oil crop is already commencing to yield.

I have the honor
to be
Your Most Obedt Servt
Augustus Archer Silva
Commercial Agent

To the
Hon Secretary of State
Washington
