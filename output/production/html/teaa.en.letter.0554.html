Department of State Received Apr 13 1878
No 27.

United States Consulate at Sierra Leone
March 6th 1878

Mr W. H. Randall
To department of State

Subject
Request for supply of Forms.

Abstract of Contents
Consulate almost bare of forms. Particulars of description of forms most urgently required. Request to be supplied without delay.

No 27.

United States Consulate at Sierra Leone
March 6th 1878

The honorable F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir,

I have the honor to report for the information of the Department of State that this consulate is almost bare of the forms most frequently in use, namely those required for shipping documents of all kinds, and to request to be supplied with an assortment as early as possible

I am, Sir
Your obedient Servant
W H Randall
Consul
