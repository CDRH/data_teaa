United States Consulate
Sierra Leone 14th March 1878

Sir,

I have the honor to own receipt of your dispatch No 21, dated 6th February, and in conformity with your request send you herewith copies of my dispatches Nos 17, 21, 22 and 23, the originals not having reached the Department of State.

I have the honor to remain
Sir
Your obedt Servant
W H Randall Consul

The honorable
F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State

Inclosure
Copies of Dispatches Nos 17, 21, 22 and 23.
