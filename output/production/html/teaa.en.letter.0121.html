United States Consulate
at St Paul de Loanda
23rd April 1879

Sir,

I have the honor to inform the Department that I leave this day for England via Lisbon per steamer "Benguella" in conformity with licence granted me for six months in your Despatch No. 71 of 22nd November 1878.

As Mr. Thomas the newly appointed Consul has not yet arrived, I have delivered over the Archives to the care of Mr Andrew Galbraith Colville who will act during my absence.
