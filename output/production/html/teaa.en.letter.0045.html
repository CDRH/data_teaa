Recd March 24
No 2

Washington March 23rd 1863

Sir

This day I had the Honor to receive your Despatch dated March 20th 1863 enclosing my Certificate of Appointment to the Commercial Agency of St Paul De Loando, also my passport, for which Please receive my acknowledgements

In my Despatch Marked No 1 Dated March 17th 1863. I had the Honor to inform you that I was Born in the City of New York and was appointed from the State of Maryland.

Also that I expected to sail from Salem about or before the 1st of May next. I have received no advices since that time that indicates an earlier day of Departure

I shall leave for my station by the first opportunity, and when that occurs will acquaint you with the facts

I have the Honor to be
Most Respectfully
Your Obdt Servant
John T. Bradberry
Commercial Agt

Honorable
W. H. Seward
Secretary of State
