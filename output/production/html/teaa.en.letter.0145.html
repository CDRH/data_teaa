No 6 3

United States Consulate
St Paul de Loanda
June 23d 1882

The Hon J. C. Bancroft Davis
Asst Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to state that Mr William Stanley Bent was appointed by me this date Acting Vice United States Consul, until the return of United States Vice Consul Robert S. Newton, so that in case of my death the Seals, archives &a. of the United States would be in proper hands

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your obedient servant
L. de R. du Verge
U.S. Consul.

Wm S Bent
Sign. of Wm S. Bent
