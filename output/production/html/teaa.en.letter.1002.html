Department of State Received Nov 1 1886
No. 30

Legation of the United States
Monrovia Sept 29/86.

Mr. S. S. Sevier
To the Third Assistant Secy of State–

Subject

Information concerning the father of Lucy C. Calloway.

Sir:

I have the honor to inform you that inquires have been made concerning the whereabouts of Stephen Lynch among the oldest settlers of this place, and the name of no such a man is known.

I am Sir
  Your obedient servant
Samuel S. Sevier
Act. Consul Gen.

Hon. A. A. Adee,
Third Assistant Secy,
Washington D.C.
