Department of State Received Oct 1 1887

No 8. U.S. Consulate Series
Monrovia L'a
August 13th '87

Chas. H. J. Taylor to Hon. Jas. D. Porter

Subject

Report of Geo. E. Eminsang for 2nd quarter ending June 30th 1887, U.S. Consular ag't at Elmina, Gold Coast Col.

Enclosures

1. Summary of business.
2. Digest of the invoice book.
3. Arrivals and departures of A'a Vessels.
4. Detailed report of official service.

 

No. 8.

U.S. Consulate Gen'l Series
Monrovia Liberia
August 13th 1887

Hon. Jas. D. Porter
Ass't Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to herewith transmit the Report of George E. Eminsang U.S. Consular agent at Elmina, Cape Coast Castle, W.C. Africa, for the 2nd quarter ending June 30th 1887. I hope the report may be found to be correct. I have the honor in this correction to say, that I do not think it wise to nominate Consular agents for Cape Palmas and Grand Bassa until such time as I am permitted by the Department to visit said places, in view of present native difficulties &c., I am sir,   sir, your obedient servant
Chas. H. J. Taylor
