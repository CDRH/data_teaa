No. 11.

Consulate General of the U.S.,
Monrovia, La., Apr. 17, 1879.

Mr. J. H. Smyth to the Third Ass't Sec'y of State.

Subject:

As to the numbers and dates of Diplomatic and Consular service of dispatches.

 

No. 11.

Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, La., Apr. 17, 1879.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of your No. 4, dated March 14, 1879, as to preceding dispatches, date and series to which they belong, inclosing memorandum of Consular series.

In reply, please accept thanks for same, and assurances of purposed observance. The date of No. 7, Consular series, case of Edward Hyndman, is Jan'y 16, 1879.

I am, Sir, with assurances of Hon. Chas. Payson,
Third Ass't Sec'y of State,
Washington, D.C.   highest consideration,
Your obd't serv't,
Jno. H. Smyth,
U.S. Consul General.
