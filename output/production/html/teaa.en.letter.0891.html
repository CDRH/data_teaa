Kenosha, Wis., May 23rd 1862.

F. W. Seward Esq.,
Assist. Sec of State;

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your esteemed favor, of the 20th[?] inst., stating the approval of my official bond, and enclosing the certificate of my appointment, as U.S. Commercial Agent, of Monrovia; together with sundry blank forms for my instructions.

My Passport was not enclosed as stated.

Herein, I enclose my Oath of Allegiance, duly attested

I have the honor to remain,
Dear Sir,
Your Obedient Servant,
Abm Hanson.
