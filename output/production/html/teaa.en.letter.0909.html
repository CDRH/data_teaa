U.S. Commercial Agency,
Monrovia, June 10, 1863.

To the Hon. W. H. Seward,
Sec. of State, of U.S. of America,

Sir,

I have the honor to enclose herein, duplicate copies of the "Liberia Herald": and beg leave to call your attention to "An Act Confining and Restricting Foreign Vessels to Ports of Entry," "from and after the first day of January A.D. 1865."

I have the honor to be,
With very sincere respect,
Your Obedient Servant,
Abm Hanson
U.S. Com. Agent.
