Department of State Received Nov 8 1875

United States Consulate
Sierra Leone
October 5th 1875

Sir,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your communication No 4 dated August 30th informing me that my Official bond has been approved and that I may shortly expect my Exequator.

The volume entitled Commercial Relation 1874 I have also received with thanks.

I have to request you will instruct the Department of State to forward to this Office the necessary forms for issuing United States Bills of Health which I am at present without.

I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your most Obedient Servant James Frame
Acting United S. Consul. France

W. Hunter Esquire
Actg Secretary
