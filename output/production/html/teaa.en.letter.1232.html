Consulate Series

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia Liberia Oct. 28, 1895

William H. Heard
To the Department of State.

Subject

Inventory of Mr. John Harris' property.

 

Consulate Series

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia Liberia Oct. 28/95

Hon. Edwin F. Uhl
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to enclose inventory of the Estate of the late John Harris of Dallas Texas. I proceed to settle the Estate as promptly as possible.

I am obediently
William H. Heard
Min. Res. and Con. Gen.
