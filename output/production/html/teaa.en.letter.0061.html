Recd Oct 6

United States Commercial Agency
St Paul de Loanda July 28th 1865

Sir

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of my Consular Bond returned to me by the Department for insertion of name as principal and to have affixed thereto an Internal Revenue stamp of one Dollar.

I expected my friends in Boston would have completed the omission now pointed out to me when said bond was in their hands to sign as bondmen.

This Despatch with the bond enclosed completed will be Hon William H Seward
