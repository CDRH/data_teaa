Department of State Received Jun 26 1886

United States Consulate
Loanda 1st May 1886.

The Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir

I have the honor to inform the Department that I arrived here in the S/S Portugal and have this day resumed my duties as Vice Consul for the United States of America in this city

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your Obedient Servant
Robt S. Newton
Vice Consul
