Index Bureau Dept of State Rec'd Nov 22 '99

No. 38, Consular Series.
Legation of the United States,
Monrovia Liberia;
Oct. 20, 1899.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit, herewith, the intelligence, that Her Brittannic Majestys Ship, Barracuta, R. H. Peirce, Commander, from Freetown, Sierra Leone, reached this Port, in the afternoon of Tuesday– Oct. 17, 1899, and sailed in the afternoon of Thursday– Oct. 19, 1899, for Accra, Gold Coast Colony.

I have the honor to be
your obedient servant;
Owen L. W. Smith,
M. R. and C. Gen'l

Hon. David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State.
