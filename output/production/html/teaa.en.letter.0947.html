Consulate General of the United States,
Monrovia, Liberia
30 August 1877.

J Milton Turner

To the Department of State,
Washington

Subject:

Requisition for Stationery

Abstract of contents:

Request for Dispatch paper and to be supplied with one large and one small Flag.

 

No 30.

Consulate General of the United States
Monrovia, Liberia
30 August 1877.

Sir;

I have the honor to request the Department to send a supply of Despatch Paper to this point, as there is not more than sufficient for the necessary uses of this post until the supply requested above can reach hither form Washington.

I hope to have the honor to thank the Department for one large and one small American Flag

I Hon F. W. Seward,
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington   I have to be,
With great respect,
Your obedient servant
J Milton Turner
