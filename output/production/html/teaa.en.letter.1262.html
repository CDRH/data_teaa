No. 23, Consular Series.
Legation of the United States,
Mar. 16, 1899.

Sir:

I have the honor to acknowledge, the receipt, this A. M. of your No. 14, Consular of Jan. 31, 1899, with inclosures, relative to four indigent American children, in Liberia.

I have to inform you that one of the four children, having died, there are now, only, three surviving.

I have the honor to be
your obedient servant;
Owen L. W. Smith,
M. R. and C. General
To
Hon. David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State,
Washington.
