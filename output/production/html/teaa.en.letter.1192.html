Department of State Received Oct 8 1892
No. 14. Consulate-General, U. S. A.,
Monrovia, Liberia, September 7, 1892 Mr William D. McCoy,
To the Department of State. SUBJECT:

Submission of "The Liberia Gazette".

ABSTRACT OF CONTENTS.

Inclosure–
1. Copy of "The Liberia Gazette".

  No. 14. Consulate-General, U. S. A.,
Monrovia, Liberia, September 7, 1892 Honorable William F. Wharton,
Assistant Secretary of State,
WASHINGTON, D. C. Sir:

I have the honor and pleasure of stating that Liberia now has a newspaper a copy of which I inclose.

It is published by and under the direction of this Government.

As there are a number of important articles in this issue I send the entire paper.

Very truly–
Your obedient servant,

William D. McCoy,
Consul-General.
