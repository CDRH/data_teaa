July 5th 1879

Hon F. W. Seward
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir,

I have the honor to transmit herewith inclosed Aggregate account of Fees for the year ending Dec 31st 1878. There being no account of fees in the books since Dec 31st 1877; I had nothing to refer to and was under the impression the account should be sent at the end of the year from the time I entered upon my duties.

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your Obt Servant
Jno. A Parm U.S. Consul
