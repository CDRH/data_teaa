United States Consulate
Sierra Leone June 12, 1873

Sir,

I beg to acknowledge to receipt of your dispatch No 5 dated 14 March in which you desire careful attention to the Consular regulations copies of which I have received some time.

Also I have received the last published list of Diplomatic and Consular Officers of the United States resident abroad.

I am,
Sir,
Your obedient Servant
William Hogan
United States Consul

The Honble
J. C. B. Davis
Assistant Secretary
Department of State
Washington
