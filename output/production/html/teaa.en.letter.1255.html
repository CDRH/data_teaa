Index Bureau Dept of State Rec'd Feb 20 '99

No. 16 Consulate Series.
Legation of the United States
Monrovia Liberia,
Jan. 17, 1899.

Sir:

I have the honor to transmit to you the intelligence, that I have herewith transmitted, to the Secretary of State a Photograph of United States Legation at Monrovia.

As the second floor of the Legation is not occupied, neither as offices nor archives of the Legation, I do hereby ask permission to occupy it as a resident for the Minister and his family.

I have the honor to be your obedient servant
Owen L. W. Smith
To Hon. David J. Hill,
Assistant Secretary of State
