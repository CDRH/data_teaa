Received Department of State Aug 25 1869

Commercial Agency of the United States of America at Sierra Leone 24th July 1869.

I have to report to the Department the death of John C. Berry a citizen of Boston, Mass: and late Master of the American Brig "Ann Elizabeth" of Salem, Mass:, which took place on the 13th instant.

I have taken an Inventory of the deceased's effects, which accompanies them by the same Brig.
