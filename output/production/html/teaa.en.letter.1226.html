William H. Heard

Subject

Request for supplies

 

No 3

Consular Series

Consulate General of United States
Monrovia Liberia May 6, 1895

To the Hon. Edwin H. Uhle
Assistant Secretary of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor to most respectfully ask for the following supplies for this office

2 Letter files
Sealing Wax
Diplomatic and Consular Envelopes, Medium
Blank Drafts, Duplicates preferred
Office table, Desk with Pigeon Holes,
Office Chair, Blanks for transit Account, Blanks Receipts for [unclear, one word], Joint Certificates for Outgoing   and Incoming Offices?

I am obediently
William H. Heard
Minister Resident and Con. General
