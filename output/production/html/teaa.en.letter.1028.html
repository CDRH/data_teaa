Department of State Received Apr 9 1887

No. 57.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia March 9/87

Mr. Sam'l S Sevier
To the Sec'y of State.

Subject

Receipt of Departments Separate of Jany 14th/87

 

No. 57.

U.S. Consulate General
Monrovia March 9/87.

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Sec'y of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have the honor respectfully to acknowledge the receipt of your "Separate" of Jany 14, 1887. In reply I have to state that no precious metals are mined in this Republic, nor is there any coinage of metals of any kind. All Treasury notes of the national banks of the United States are at par throughout this Republic   Republic.

I am, Sir,
Your obedient servant
Samuel S. Sevier
U.S. Vice Consul General

Hon. T. F. Bayard
Sec'y of State
Washington D.C.
