Index Bureau Dept of State Received May 4 93
No. 20. Consulate-General U.S.A.,
Monrovia, Liberia, March 22, 1893 Mr William D. McCoy,
To the Department of State. SUBJECT:

Request for two copies of Rules and Regulations governing the U.S. Navy.

ABSTRACT OF CONTENTS.   No. 20. Consulate-General, U.S.A.,
Monrovia, Liberia, March 22, 1893 Honorable
Assistant Secretary of State,
WASHINGTON, D. C. Sir:

The Liberian Government has requested a copy of the Rules and Regulations governing the U.S. Navy.

Will you please have two copies mailed – one for the Liberian Government and one to be placed on file in this office.

Your obedient Servant,

William D. McCoy,
U.S. Consul-General.
