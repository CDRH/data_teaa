Asking for information regarding the duties of Destitute Seamen sent to the U.S. in American Ships under the law. ($10 each)

No 40. U.S. Consulate at
Sierra Leone Dec. 20th 1880.

Hon John Hay
Ass't. Secty of State
Washington D.C.

Sir:

I have to ask for information regarding the duties of Destitute Seamen sent to the United States in American Vessels under the law ($10 each)

Are they subject to orders & duty same as sailors regularly shipped? Are 1st Mates and 2nd Mates send under the law subject to duty same as ordinary sailors?

I find no instructions covering these points in the Regulations.

I am Sir
Your Obt Servant
Judson A. Lewis
U.S. Consul
