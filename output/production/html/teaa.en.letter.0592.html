Consulate of the United States of America at Bathurst.
24th June 1859

Sir,

I have the honor to receive your despatch dated the 7th March 1859, informing me that the President had been good enough to promote me to the office of Consul, instead of Commercial Agent.

The bond has been signed by me and provided to New York, for signatures of bondsmen, and will no doubt soon reach you.
